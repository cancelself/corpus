15. bahuśruta vagga

Brough 246' data-uid='gdhp246prahodi duhiṇo dokhu

Brough 247' data-uid='gdhp247suhidasa vi pramoju

dukhavaśamakamia ◦

Brough 248' data-uid='gdhp248śilamada mahapraña
dharmakamabahoṣuda
bhayidavi saprañeṇa

Brough 249' data-uid='gdhp249bahuṣuda dhamadhara

ta bhayea tadhavidha ◦

Brough 250' data-uid='gdhp250sabhir ava samasea

Brough 251' data-uid='gdhp251yadha akara suchana

Brough 252' data-uid='gdhp252emam eva idhekacu

Brough 253' data-uid='gdhp253pradiveṇa nu ruvani

Brough 254' data-uid='gdhp254suṣuda ṣudavaḍhaṇa

Brough 255' data-uid='gdhp255so arthaladhu medhavi
