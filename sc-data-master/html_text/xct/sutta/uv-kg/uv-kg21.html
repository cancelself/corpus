21. Tathāgatha

D vp 227b1

D vp 227b1-7Verse 431Verse 21.1 ’jig rten ’di na kun rig kun zil gnon ||
’di na chos rnams kun gyis ma gos śiṅ ||
kun spaṅs sred zad rab tu rnam grol ba ||
ñid kyis mṅon mkhyen pa la su yis bstan ||

Verse 432Verse 21.2de bźin gśegs pa lha mi’i ston pa ste ||
stobs rnams kun ldan thams cad mkhyen pa ṅa ||
byaṅ chub brñes nas ñid kyis rab ston pa ||
mtshuṅs med mnam med ṅa la su yis bstan ||

Verse 433Verse 21.3ṅa ni ’jig rten dgra bcom ste ||
’jig rten dag na bla med daṅ ||
lha daṅ bcas pa’i ’jig rten na ||
bdud rnams zil gnon rgyal ba ṅa ||

Verse 434Verse 21.4ṅa daṅ ’dra ba med pas na ||
ṅa la slob dpon su yaṅ med ||
’jig rten ’di na gcig pu yis ||
rdzogs pa’i byaṅ chub bla med brñes ||

Verse 435Verse 21.5gaṅ źig zag pa zad brñes pa ||
rgyal ba ṅa ’drar śes par gyis ||
sdig pa’i chos las ṅa rgyal bas ||
de bas ñe ’gro ṅa rgyal ba ||

Verse 436Verse 21.7’jigs rten yul sred rgal ba yi ||
kun mkhyen saṅs rgyas mya ṅan ’das ||
’jig rten pa la ma bstan par ||
kun nas ’byuṅ ba mi śes pas ||

Verse 437Verse 21.6ba ra ṇa ser soṅ nas su ||
ṅas ni chos kyi rṅa brduṅ źiṅ ||
’jig rten sus kyaṅ ma bskor ba’i ||
chos kyi ’khor lo bskor bar byas ||

Verse 438Verse 21.8dpa’ bo de bźin gśegs pa rnams ||
’di na chos kyis ’dul bar mdzad ||
de dag chos kyis btul ba la ||
mkhas pa su yaṅ smod mi byed ||

Verse 439Verse 21.9ab10brtan gaṅ tiṅ ’dzin la źugs śiṅ ||
ṅes ’byuṅ źi la dga’ ba daṅ ||
sku yi ta ma ’dzin pa yi ||
rdzogs pa’i saṅs rgyas grags ldan pa ||
śes rab myur ba de dag la ||
lha daṅ mi rnams dga’ bar byed ||

D vp 227b7-228a6Verse 440Verse 21.11 gaṅ dag ’das pa’i saṅs rgyas daṅ ||
gaṅ dag ma byon saṅs rgyas daṅ ||
gaṅ dag da ltar rdzogs saṅs rgyas ||
maṅ po’i mya ṅan sel mdzad pa ||

Verse 441Verse 21.12bźugs par gyur ciṅ bźugs pa daṅ ||
de bźin bźugs par ’gyur kun gyis ||
dam chos bla mar mdzad pa ’di ||
rdzogs saṅs rgyas kyi chos ñid yin ||

Verse 442Verse 21.13de bas ’di na bdag dga’ źiṅ ||
bdag ñid chen po mṅon ’dod pas ||
saṅs rgyas bstan pa dran byas te ||
dam pa’i chos la bya mar bya ||

Verse 443Verse 21.14mi gaṅ saṅs rgyas bstan pa la ||
dad pa ma bskyed byis pa de ||
rgud pa’i gnas su ’gro ’gyur te ||
srin mo rnams kyis tshod pa bźin ||

Verse 444Verse 21.15mi gaṅ saṅs rgyas bstan pa la ||
dad pa bskyed pa śes rab can ||
de ni bde bar pha rol ’gro ||
smrin śugs ldan pas tshoṅ pa bźin ||

Verse 445Verse 21.16de bźin gśegs pa mñam med mtshuṅs pa med ||
bde bar rnam rtog de bźin rab dben pa’i ||
rnam rtog gñis po spyod pa’i saṅs rgyas rnams ||
mun bsal pha rol gśegs śiṅ grags daṅ ldan ||

Verse 446Verse 21.17des ni brñes bya brñes śiṅ yid dbaṅ gyur ||
kun grol zag zad rab tu rnam par grol ||
rnam grol źugs ldan tha ba zag med rnams ||
lus can ’jig rten rnams la phan phyir gzigs ||

Verse 447Verse 21.18’ga’ źig ri bo’i spo la ’dug nas su ||
’gro ba rnams la kun du lta ba ltar ||
de bźin blo bzaṅ mya ṅan bral ba dag ||
chos las byuṅ pa’i khaṅ bzaṅs steṅ gśegs nas ||
skye bo skye rgas zil gnon mya ṅan gyis ||
gduṅs pa rnams la kun du spyan gzigs nas ||
’chi ba med pa’i sgo mo ’byed mdzad kyis ||
gaṅ dag ñan ’dod the tsom bzal bar gyis ||

de bźin gśegs pa’i tshoms te ñi śu gcig pa’o ||
