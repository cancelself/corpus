Types of Parallels

Icons for indicating relations

We use Material Design icons to indicate various relations between the parallels.

Icon Definition Explanation
Full parallel Indicates different versions of the same text.
Resembling parallel Indicates that texts are comparable.
Mentions One text is quoted or mentioned in another.
Retelling A story or event is “backed up in cache” or “recycled” by being retold in another place.
