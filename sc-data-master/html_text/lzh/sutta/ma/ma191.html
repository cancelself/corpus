中阿含雙品第一竟（萬一千五百八十字）（第五後誦）

This Madhyamāgama (中阿含經 ) of the Sarvāstivāda school was translated in the Eastern Jin dynasty in 397–398 CE by Gautama Saṁghadeva (僧伽提婆) and a team including Saṅgharakṣa as reciter and Daoci, Libao, and Kanghua as scribes.

The text is from CBETA, which is based on the Taishō edition of the Chinese canon.
