• bhikṣuṇī-prakīrṇaka 16

strī-tīrtha-pratisaṁyuktaṁ

Origin story

SC 1 Roth 269bhagavān śrāvastīyam viharati | tāyo dāni bhikṣuṇīyo strī-tīrthe ānīcolaṁ dhovanti | striyo dāni ojjhāyanti | sarvam idam tīrthaṁ rudhireṇa viṭṭālitaṁ | etaṁ prakaraṇaṁ | peyālaṁ | yāvad bhagavān āha |

The ruling

SC 2 tena hi na kṣamati stri-tīrthe ānīcolakaṁ dhovituṁ | strī-tīrtha-pratisaṁyuktaṁ |

Cases

SC 3 sā eṣā bhikṣuṇī ānīcolakaṁ strī-tīrthe dhovati vinayātikramam āsādayati | idam ucyate strī-tīrtha-pratisaṁyuktaṁ || phu ||
