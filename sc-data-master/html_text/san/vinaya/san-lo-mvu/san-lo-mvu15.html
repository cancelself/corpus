15. The Eighth Bhūmi

SC 1evam ukte āyuṣmān mahākāśyapa āyuṣmantaṁ mahākātyāyanam uvāca || bhagavatā bho jinaputra samyaksaṁbuddhena śākyamuninā prathamādvitīyātṛtīyācaturthāpaṁcamāṣaṣṭhāsaptamāsu bhūmiṣu vartamānena yeṣu
