7. The Ten Bhūmis

SC 1atra daśabhūmiko kartavyo dīpaṁkaravastu ca ||

nāmo ’stu buddhānāṁ namo ’rhatāṁ ||

SC 2daśabhūmikasyādi

SC 3vatte apratima dharmadarśanaṁ
Sen 1.64 na ekakalpaśatasaṁcitātmanāṁ |
bhūmayo daśa jināna śrīmatāṁ
yair vikurviṣu sadā paṇḍitā ||

SC 4mānadarpamadamohamocitā
sarvaśaḥ samiyamārdavānvitā |
gauravaṁ janiya sarvadarśiṣu
śrūyatāṁ jinavarasya śāsanaṁ ||

SC 5nirvṛte kanakarāśīsannibhe
śākyanandijanane tathāgate |
kaṁpi medini saśailakānanā
sāgarāṁbaradharā saparvatā ||

SC 6kampitaṁ paramaromaharṣaṇaṁ
bhūmikaṁpam anudṛśya dāruṇaṁ |
kāśyapo dhūtaguṇāgrapārago
cittam abhyupagataḥ tadā abhūt* ||

SC 7kiṁ nu adya dharaṇī saparvatā
sāgarāmbaradharā vasundharā |
kampate paramadāruṇasvaraṁ
nūnaṁ nirvṛtiṁ gataḥ tathāgataḥ ||

SC 8so ca divyanayanas tathāgataṁ
devakinnaravarehi vanditaṁ
Sen 1.65 dṛśya sarvabhavabandhanāntakaṁ
nirvṛtaṁ yamakaśāla-antare ||

SC 9na khalu me samucitaṁ tathāgataṁ
ṛddhiye samanugantu gautamaṁ |
padbhir eva vadatāṁ varaṁ muniṁ
draṣṭum apratimaṁ pravrajāmy ahaṁ ||

SC 10so ca matva tvaramāṇo sūrī
kāśyapo mathitamānaso bhikṣuḥ |
bhikṣubhiḥ bahubhiḥ uttamo pari-
nirvṛtabhuvam anupūrvam āgami ||

SC 11tasya ca praṇidhir āsi uttamā
kāśyapasya jinapādavandane |
tau + + + caraṇau mahāmune
mūrdhinā upanipīḍya vandituṁ ||

SC 12saṁgṛhyolkāṁ vipulāṁ atha catasro
mallakā upagatā balavantaḥ |
vījitāṁ paramamallavinītāṁ
dagdhaḍalkāṁ abhiprāṇamayensuḥ ||

SC 13taiś citām abhimukhaṁ upanītā
taiḥ parākramabalai rathaśūraiḥ |
Sen 1.66 nirvṛtā ca + + + sā samakālaṁ
prāpya vāripariṣekam ivolkā ||

SC 14saṁśayaṁ vimatimadhyam upetya
mallā divyanayanaṁ aniruddhaṁ |
gauravā nataśirā suvinītā
praśnam* + + + idaṁ paripṛcche ||

SC 15ko nu hetur iha pratyayo ca ko
yen’ imā jinasutā upanītā |
nirvṛtiṁ upagatā sahasolkā
brūhi kāraṇam ihārya yathāvat* ||

SC 16devatā khalu prasannā kāśyape
tasya eṣa khalu ṛddhibhāvanā |
naiva tāva jvalano jvaliṣyati
yāva nāgato agrapāragaḥ ||

SC 17tasya caiṣa praṇidhiḥ samṛdhyati
kāśyapasya dhutadharmadhāriṇaḥ |
tau kramau daśabalasya śrīmataḥ
vandituṁ hi śirasā mahāmune ||

SC 18so ca bhikṣugaṇasaṁpuraskṛtaḥ
kāśyapo dhutarajo jinātmajaḥ |
prāṁjalī jinacitām upāgato
gauravā praṇataśīrṣamānasaḥ ||

SC 19Sen 1.67 dṛśya taṁ pravararūpadhāriṇaṁ
kāṣṭhasaṁcayagataṁ tathāgataṁ |
dhig bhavān iti girām udīrayī
darśitaprakṛtibhāvalakṣaṇāṁ ||

SC 20ko nu so bhavam upetya prāṇako
yo na mṛtyuvaśam āgamiṣyati |
yatr’ ayaṁ jvalanakāṁcanopamo
nirvṛto śikhir ivendhanaṁ vinā ||

SC 21kṛtva aṁjalipuṭaṁ mahāyaśo
pādato jinavarasya kāśyapo |
mūrdhinā nipatito maharṣiṇo
paścimaṁ idaṁ namasyate mune ||

SC 22tau ca cakravaralakṣitau kramau
devadānavavarehi vanditau |
niḥsṛtau tatha vidārya tāṁ citāṁ
devayakṣabhujagānubhāvitau ||

SC 23tau kramau śirasi sannipātiya
pāṇibhiḥ samanugṛhya cā mune |
ālape śrutidharaṁ maharṣiṇaṁ
antikāvacaraḥ kāśyapas tadā ||

SC 24Sen 1.68 kiṁ tv imau śrutidhara kramau mune
dhyāmatām upagatau na suprabhau |
brūhi kāraṇam aśeṣam āhvaya
yen’ imau na nayanābhinandinau ||

SC 25etaṁ śrutva śrutasaṁcayaṁdharo
kāśyapaṁ idam uvāca paṇḍito |
aśruvegaduṣitā va śocatāṁ
roditehi mathitā h’ imau kramau ||

SC 26ten’ imau kramavarau mahāmuneḥ
rodanena janatābhipīḍitau |
no vibhānti munino yathā purā
evam etad anupaśya suvrata ||

SC 27so nipatya śirasā punaḥ punaḥ
tau kramau pravaracakralakṣaṇau |
karatalehi abhipīḍayet mune
śāstu gauravaparāya buddhiye ||

SC 28vanditau ca dhutadharmadhāriṇā
tau kramau guṇadhareṇa śāstuno |
lokanāthacitakāṣṭha tejasā
vāyuvegavidhutena dīpyati ||

SC 29Sen 1.69 dahyamāne jinacandraśarīre
paṁca tāni vaśibhūtaśatāni |
mantrayanti sahitā samupetya
nirvṛtīsamayakāle saṁgītiṁ ||

SC 30nirvṛto pravaralakṣaṇadhārī
yo nu śāstu sasurāsuranetā |
ko guṇo iha ciraṁ parivāse
vayam api adya vijahāmatha dehaṁ ||

SC 31sarvathā supariniṣṭhitakāryāḥ
prāpya acyutam aśokam anantaṁ |
sarvabhāvabhavavītigatā sma
eṣa nirvṛtim upema ihaiva ||

SC 32evam ukte dhutadharmaviśuddho
kāśyapo ’bravi tadā vaśibhūtāṁ |
na khu bhavadbhiḥ anupādi vimukti
nirvṛtī samanugamya ihaiva ||

SC 33tīrthikā ca bahidhānugatāś ca
kreyur apratimaśāsanadoṣaṁ |
dhūmakālikam iti śramaṇasya
etat eva ca tu rakṣaṇīyaṁ no ||

SC 34lokanātha bahavo narasiṁhā
Sen 1.70 ye c’ anāgata mahāmatiśūrā |
te hi no upapadeyur udagrā
yadi na saṁkaliye śāsanaṁ śāstuḥ ||

SC 35tena apratihatāḥ susamagrāḥ
gāyathā sugataśāsanam agryaṁ |
yathā idaṁ suparigīta yathārthaṁ
cirataraṁ naramarūṣu viroce ||

SC 36evam astu iti te vaśibhūtā
kāśyapasya vacanaṁ pratipūjya |
cittam apy upagatā kva idānīṁ
deśi dharmadharasaṁgaṇanā syāt* ||

SC 37ramyakānanavane susamṛddhe
māgadhasya magadhādhipatisya |
puravare bhavatu rājagṛhasmiṁ
saptaparṇaabhidhānaguhāyāṁ ||

SC 38parvatasya vaihāyavarasya
uttarasmi tīre varapārśve |
vividhapādape śilātalabhūmeḥ
bhāge yaṁ bhavatu dharmasamāsyā ||

SC 39te ca ṛddhivaśibhāvabalasthā
Sen 1.71 udgatāḥ khagapathe jinaputrā |
tatkṣaṇāntara + + + prapalānā
mānasaṁ saro yathā haṁsayūtho ||

SC 40te pratiṣṭhitā nagāgravarasya
pārśve tat vanam upetya niṣaṇṇā |
śāsane ca sugatasya sugīte
devadundubhigaṇāni nadensuḥ ||

SC 41te ca dundubhina nādaṁ nadantaṁ
śrutva śāsanakarā sugatasya |
bhūmikampam anudṛśya ca ghoraṁ
kāśyapaṁ dhutarajaṁ idam ūcuḥ ||

SC 42kin tu bhoḥ dhutadharā samakaṁpi
medinī sasaritā sasamudrā |
devadundubhiravāś ca manojñā
divyamālyavikiraṇaṁ ca bhavanti ||

SC 43tān uvāca dhutadharmasamaṁgī
kāśyapo jinasutāṁ vaśibhūtāṁ |
ete sannipatitā marusaṁghā
śrutva śāsanavaraṁ susamagryaṁ ||

SC 44te samagravaralakṣaṇadhārī
gauravāt pramuditā marusaṁghāḥ |
pūjāṁ apratimakasya karonti
Sen 1.72 śāsanaṁ śṛṇuyu sarvasamagryaṁ ||

SC 45so hi ’nekabhavakalpaśatehi
hitasukhāya naradevagaṇānāṁ |
evam abhyupagato cirarātraṁ
mokṣayiṣye ’haṁ prajāḥ parimuktaḥ ||

SC 46so labhitva paramārtham aśokaṁ
sarvabhāvabhavaduḥkhanirodhaṁ |
kāśipuryāṁ naradevahitārthaṁ
vartayiṣyi varacakram adbhutaṁ ||

SC 47paṁcakehi saha tehi munīhi
marugaṇāṁ vinayavādināṁ varo |
satvakoṭinayutāni nāyako
jātijanmamaraṇāt samuddhare ||

SC 48so vimokṣayi bhavābhinandino
toṣayaṁ naramarū narasiṁho |
mathiya sarvaparavādi sāṁprataṁ
nirvṛto tu bhagavāṁ nirapekṣo ||

SC 49eta śrutva vacanaṁ manoramaṁ
kāśyapasya dhutadharmadhāriṇo |
devasaṁghā muditā nabhe sthitā
vyāharanti vacanaṁ manoramaṁ ||

SC 50sādhu sādhu dhutadharmakovidā
Sen 1.73 śāstu śāsanakarā ananyathā |
bhāṣase guṇam anantabuddhino
jetavane naramarū sutoṣitā ||

SC 51so hi devamanujāna uttamo
so hi agrapuruṣo mahāmuniḥ |
so hi niḥśaraṇam uttamaṁ prabhuḥ
dṛṣṭadarśano hitāya prāṇināṁ ||

SC 52tena skandhā taḍibudbudopamā
phenapiṇḍakam iva prabhāsvaraṁ |
deśitā daśabalena jānatā
yasy’ iyaṁ guṇakathā pravartate ||

SC 53kṛṣṇasarpaśirasannibhās tathā
kāma agrapuruṣeṇa deśitā |
śastrarucchaviṣakumbhasannibhā
yasy’ iyaṁ guṇakathā pravartate ||

SC 54tena dṛṣṭam acalaṁ paraṁ sukhaṁ
dṛṣṭibhiḥ paramasādhudṛṣṭibhiḥ |
taṁ amatsaravatā prakāśitaṁ
saṁvibhāgarucinā yad ad bhutaṁ ||

SC 55udgate dinakare yathā kimi
Sen 1.74 niṣprabho bhavati no ca bhrājati |
udgate jinadivākare tathā
niṣprabhā paragaṇī asaṁyatā ||

SC 56ṛddhipādabalapāramiṁ gato
īśvaro jinabalena cakṣumāṁ |
locanaṁ bhagavatasya paśyatha
nirvṛto kanakabimbasannibho ||

SC 57dhig bhavāṁ sarada-abhrasannibhā
vālikānagararūpasannibhā |
yatra nāma kuśalāna saṁcayo
nirvṛto paramabuddhisāgaro ||

SC 58hetukāraṇaśatehi nāyako
nardate puruṣasiṁhanarditaṁ |
bhavam ananyamaraṇaṁ nirīkṣya
tasya uktam antaraṁ na vidyate ||

SC 59divyapuṣpavaramaṇḍitaṁ nabhaṁ
śobhate sugatavarṇabhāṣaṇe |
divyacandanarasānuvāsitaṁ
śobhate amṛtagandhikaṁ nabham iti ||

SC 60atha khalv āyuṣmān mahāmaudgalyāyano āyuṣmantaṁ mahākāśyapam āmantrayati || vyavasthāpaya jinaputra vaśibhūtā ye pariṣāyāṁ saṁśayagatāni mānasāni vijāneyur iti ||

SC 61Sen 1.75 athāniruddhaṁ upāliṁ ca sthaviraṁ ca alakuṇḍalabhaṭṭiyaṁ sundaranandaṁ ca kāśyapa uvāca ||

SC 62avaloketha jinātmajāś cittāni yā pariṣāyāṁ |
saṁśayaṁ ca paripṛcchatha yasya yatra tathā bhaved iti ||

SC 63sādhū ti te pratiśrutvā jinaśāstraviśāradāḥ |
paśyanti paracittāni kare vāmalakaṁ yathā ||

SC 64pralambabāhuṁ vaśibhūtaṁ kāśyapo idam abravīt* |
gṛdhrakūṭasya śikhare nirmiṇe vasudhāṁ laghuṁ ||

SC 65aṣṭādaśa sahasrāṇi pariṣāyāṁ samāgatā |
yathā sarvābhijāneyā ṛddhiṁ saṁjanayā tathā ||

SC 66vicintacūtaṁ vaśibhūtaṁ kāśyapa idam abravīt* |
gaṁgodakamayā meghā nirmiṇe gagane laghuṁ ||

SC 67vividhagandhapuṣpāś ca upavāyantu sarvataḥ |
mānuṣāṇām āmagandhā ca śīghram antarahāpaya ||

SC 68haryakṣaṁ nāma vaśibhūtaṁ kāśyapa idam abravīt* |
tathā utpādaya śīghraṁ samādhiṁ sugatātmaja |
yathā gṛhīṇāṁ dravyāṇi na gaccheyuḥ parāṁ gatiṁ ||

SC 69varuṇaṁ nāma vaśibhūtaṁ kāśyapo idam abravīt* |
aratidaṁśamaśakā manuṣyāṇāṁ nivartaye ||

SC 70Sen 1.76 ajakarṇaṁ vaśibhūtaṁ kāsyapo idam abravīt* |
kṣudhāṁ pipāsāṁ vyādhiṁ ca manuṣyāṇāṁ nivartaya ||

SC 71sādhū ti te pratiśrutvā kāśyapasya jinātmajāḥ |
yathājñaptāni sthānāni yathoktaṁ parijāgriṣu ||

SC 72tataḥ kāśyapasthaviraḥ kātyāyanam uvāca sa |
samudīraya mahātmanāṁ caritaṁ dharmarājināṁ ||

SC 73evam ukte mahāprājño kātyāyanakulodgataḥ |
uvāca caryā buddhānāṁ kāśyapasya anupṛcchataḥ ||

SC 74śruyatāṁ bho jinasutā buddhānāṁ sarvadarśināṁ |
caryā caraṇaśuddhānāṁ yathāvat anupūrvaśaḥ ||

SC 75daśa khalu bho jinaputrā bodhisatvāna bhūmayo |
+ + + + + + + + + bhavanti katamā daśa ||

SC 76durāroheti prathamā bhūmī samupadiśyate |
dvitīyā baddhamānā nāma tṛtīyā puṣpamaṇḍitā ||

SC 77caturthī rucirā nāma paṁcamī cittavistarā |
ṣaṣṭī rūpavatī nāma saptamī durjayā smṛtā ||

SC 78aṣṭamā janmanideśo navamī yauvarājyato |
daśamī tv abhiṣekāto iti etā daśa bhūmayaḥ ||

SC 79Sen 1.77 evam ukte tu gāthābhiḥ kāśyapo ’bravi paṇḍitaḥ |
kātyāyanam ato ’tyarthaṁ vākyam apratimaṁ idaṁ ||

SC 80bhūmīnāṁ pariṇāmāni yathāvat anukīrtaya |
yathā ca te vivartante saṁsaranto mahāyaśā ||

SC 81yathā cāpi saṁvartante sattvasārāḥ tathā vade |
yathaivādhyāśayā teṣāṁ bhavanti tāṁ udāhare ||

SC 82yathā ca parikalpenti satvā satvasamanvitā |
yathā ca denti dānāni tat sarvam anukīrtaye ||

SC 83tathā ca dṛṣṭvā saṁbuddhā bhāṣanto ca manojanaṁ |
saṁjñotpādaṁ tathā brūhi kāśyapo idam abravīt* ||

SC 84idaṁ te vacanaṁ śrutvā vaśibhūtā upasthitā |
gauraveṇa mahāsatvā saṁbuddhānāṁ mahātmanāṁ ||

SC 85evam ukte kātyāyano kāśyapam uvāca || na khalu bho jinaputra śakyaṁ bodhisatvānāṁ bhūmīḥ pramātuṁ ettakakalpā vā anantā bhavanti | sarvaṁ saṁsāro bodhisatvānāṁ khaṇḍasaṁjñayā bhūmir iti parikalpitaṁ tena bhūmir iti smṛtā || evam ukte āyuṣmān ānanda āyuṣmantaṁ kātyāyanam uvāca || yadi bho jinaputra ekā bhūmi aprameyā Sen 1.78 katham idānīṁ pariśeṣāṇāṁ bhūmīnāṁ grahaṇaṁ bhaviṣyati iti || evam ukte āyuṣmān kātyāyano āyuṣmantam ānandaṁ gāthābhir adhyabhāṣe ||

SC 86kalpo yathā aparimitaḥ prakāśitaḥ
prajānatā avitathavādinā svayaṁ |
kalpeṣu ca bhavati bahūsu deśanā
idaṁ nu bho puruṣavarasya śāsanaṁ ||

SC 87bhūmis tathā aparimitā prajānatā
prakāśitā svayam anivṛttabuddhinā |
pravartate tatha pariśeṣabhūmiṣu
sāmānyasaṁketānāṁ nirūpaṇaṁ ||
