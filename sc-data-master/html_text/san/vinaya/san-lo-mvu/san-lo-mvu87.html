87. The Story of Sarvaṁdada

SC 1mahāntaṁ dharmasaṁbhāraṁ mahājanahitāvahaṁ |
mahāvastuṁ mahājñānaṁ bhadantasugatena hi |
sarvasatvānāṁ hitāya saṁbuddhena prakāśitaṁ ||

SC 2ye ca deśenti saddharmaṁ śṛṇonti ye ca deśitaṁ |
sarve te adhigacchanti nirvāṇapadam acyutaṁ ||

SC 3sarvaṁdadasya pura jātir abhūṣi siddhā
ācāradānadamathair jagatīhiteṣī |
savighnarūpacarito tad abhūṣi śakro
mā cyāvayiṣyati mameṣa ito grasthānāta ||

SC 4so nirmiṇitva narakaṁ sarvadasya tasya
ye dānaśūraguṇavāṁ prapatanti atra |
Sen 3.251 saṁhṛtya vīra vata taṁ duḥkham evarūpaṁ
dānaṁ nidānaṁ sarvasatva sukhī bhavantu ||

SC 5śakro bravīt praśamadānadamena vīra
kiṁ prārthayet* bhagavatīsukhatāṁ paratra |
so hy abravīt bhagavatīpure nāsti kāya
prāg eva kho bhagavatīpura-arthikeṣu ||

SC 6yatra na jānati jarāṁ na ca mṛtyuṁ vyādhiṁ
na ca + + + priyajanaḥ priyaviprayogaḥ |
śāntaṁ nirālayaṁ nirantaraduḥkhamokṣaṁ
prārthayāmi padam acyutaṁ nirvṛtiṁ ca ||

SC 7so hy abravīt paramaduṣkaram etat sthānaṁ
bodhīvibodhanakaṁ kāmaratiprahāṇaṁ |
nivartaya mataṁ svakaṁ janayāhi chandaṁ
kāmasukhe viṣamabhogasukhe ca ramye ||

SC 8śakrāsanaṁ yadi bhave mama nityakālaṁ
sarvaiś ca kāmaratibhiḥ na ca viprayogaṁ |
na tv eva kāmaguṇasaukhyanibaddha buddhi
sarvajñatāya praṇidhiṁ na nivartayeyaṁ ||

SC 9divyāni mānuṣasukhāni ca yāni loke
yaṁ ceha janmani sukhāsti sukhaṁ yam ūrdhvaṁ |
te kalpakoṭinayutā sukhito bhaveyaṁ
Sen 3.252 sarvajñatāye praṇidhiṁ na nivartayeyaṁ ||

SC 10duḥkhāni yāni ca ihāsti ca śrūyate ca
ye kalpakoṭinayuteṣu bahuprakāraṁ |
te duḥkha kalpanayutā duḥkhito bhaveyaṁ
sarvajñatāya praṇidhiṁ na nivartayeyaṁ ||

SC 11yāvantareṇa paramārthavidū bhaveyaṁ
tāvantaraṁ yadi avīcigato vaseyaṁ |
vīryaṁ ca me na pratisaṁharaṇāya śakyaṁ
sarvajñatāya praṇidhiṁ na nivartayeyaṁ ||

SC 12+ + + + + + + + + + + +
+ + + + + + + + + yac ca bāla |
bhuṁjeya ayoguḍaṁ pibeya vilīnalohaṁ
sarvajñatāya praṇidhiṁ na nivartayeyaṁ ||

SC 13pātālamadhye nivase vadhakehi sārdhaṁ
khajje śarīraṁ ca punaḥ puna saṁjaneya |
tad duḥkhaṁ tādṛśakam udvahituṁ samartho
sarvajñatāya praṇidhiṁ na nivartayeyaṁ ||

SC 14śaraśaktivarṣaṁ yadi varṣayi tīkṣṇadhāraṁ
sarvaṁ śarīraṁ yadi chijjati taṁ aśeṣaṁ |
tad duḥkhaṁ tādṛśakam udvahituṁ samarthaḥ
sarvajñatāya praṇidhiṁ na nivartayeyaṁ ||

SC 15Sen 3.253 yadi gaṁgavālukasameṣu karitva pūjāṁ
buddheṣu paścahaṁ labheyam imam agrabodhiṁ |
taṁ tāvad dīrghaṁ bhavaṁ saṁsārituṁ samarthaḥ
sarvajñatāya praṇidhiṁ na nivartayeyaṁ ||

SC 16garbhāsaye yadi bhave mahakalpakoṭī
jātaś ca jātiśataṁ khijjeya agaṁ me so |
tad duḥkhaṁ tādṛśaṁ śarīragataṁ saheyaṁ
sarvajñatāya praṇidhiṁ na nivartayeyaṁ ||

SC 17yāni ca duḥkhā narake tatha tiryagyonyāṁ
pretamanuṣyakabhaveṣu bahuprakāraṁ |
taṁ duḥkhaṁ sarvaṁ nipate yadi me śarīraṁ
sarvajñatāya pranidhiṁ na nivartayeyaṁ ||

SC 18yāvanti prāṇanayutā vinayeyaṁ loke
tāvanti kalpanayutāṁ narake vaseyaṁ |
taṁ duḥkhaṁ tāvaciram udvahituṁ samarthaḥ
sarvajñatāya praṇidhiṁ na nivartayeyaṁ ||

SC 19nāhaṁ punaḥ ito nivartayituṁ samarthaḥ
eṣo so niścaya kṛto jagato hitārthaṁ |
yāvan na prāptam amṛtaṁ paripūrṇamāsā
sarvajñatāya praṇidhiṁ na nivartayeyaṁ ||

SC 20saṁnāha eṣa dṛḍha vajramayo saṁnaddho
dāne dame vratatape na me asti khedo |
Sen 3.254 satvāna mocayituṁ haṁ apunaḥbhavāya
sarvajñatāye praṇidhiṁ na nivartayeyaṁ ||

SC 21evaṁ niścitamatī suviniścitārthā
bodhīnibaddhahṛdayā vicaranti loke |
laṁghitabahubhir iva pāragatā samudraṁ
prāptonti bodhiṁ sabalaṁ namuciṁ nihatvā ||
