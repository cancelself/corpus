9. The Second Bhūmi

SC 1tataś ca kāśyapasthaviraḥ mahākātyāyanam abravīt* |
nirdiṣṭā prathamā bhūmi mahāsatva manoramā ||

SC 2dvitīyāṁ saṁkramantānāṁ bhūmiṁ naravarātmajā |
kiṁ cittaṁ bodhisattvānāṁ jāyate samanantaraṁ ||

SC 3ke ca adhyāśayā santi dvitīyāyāṁ jinātmaja |
bhūmiṁ tāṁ bodhisatvānāṁ yathābhūtam udīrayed iti ||

SC 4tataḥ kātyāyanaḥ sthaviraḥ kāśyapam idam abravīt* |
nirdeśaṁ bodhisatvānāṁ kīrtayiṣye manoramaṁ ||

SC 5Sen 1.85 dvitīyāṁ saṁkramantānāṁ prathamato ’nujāyate |
aratī bodhisatvānāṁ bhaveṣv iti na saṁśayaṁ ||

SC 6bodhisatvānāṁ bho jinaputra dvitīyāyāṁ bhūmau vartamānānāṁ ime adhyāśayā bhavanti | tadyathā kalyāṇādhyāśayā bhavanti snigdhādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti madhurādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti tīkṣṇādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti vipulādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti vicitrādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti gambhīrādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti aparyādinnādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti anupahatādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti asādhāraṇādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti unnatādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti akṛpaṇādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti anivartādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti akṛtrimādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti śuddhādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti dṛḍhādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti svabhāvādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti tṛptādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti pudgalādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti anantādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti ||

SC 7kathaṁ bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvāḥ kalyāṇādhyāśayāḥ bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 8buddhe dharme ca saṁghe ca na kāṁkṣanti kathaṁcana |
iti adhyāśayas teṣāṁ kalyāṇa upadiśyate ||

SC 9kathaṁ bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvāḥ snigdhādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 10aṅgeṣu chidyamāneṣu manas teṣāṁ na kupyate |
evam adhyāśayas teṣāṁ snigdhamṛdūpadiśyate ||

SC 11kathaṁ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvāḥ madhurādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 12Sen 1.86 antaḥkuśalakarmāṇi sevanti puruṣottamāḥ |
evam adhyāśayā madhurā bhavanti dhutabuddhinām iti ||

SC 13kathaṁ bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvāḥ tīkṣṇādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 14budhyanty āśayasaṁyuktā loke lokottare tathā |
evam adhyāśayā tīkṣṇā bhavanti śuddhakarmaṇām iti ||

SC 15kathaṁ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā vipulādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 16sarvabhūtāna hitārthaṁ saṁcinonti śubhaṁ bahuṁ |
evam adhyāśayā vipulā bhavanti paramarṣiṇām iti ||

SC 17kathaṁ bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā vicitrādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 18vicitrāṇi manojñāṇi denti dānāny amatsarāḥ |
evam adhyāśayā vicitrā bhavanty uttamadarṣinām iti ||

SC 19kathaṁ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā aparyādinnādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 20aparyādinnacittās te prativedhaparākramāḥ |
evam adhyāśayas teṣām aparyādinna ucyate ||

SC 21kathaṁ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā anupahatādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 22na te śakyanti saṁhartuṁ duṣṭacittena kenacit* |
evam adhyāśayas teṣāṁ na jātu upahanyate ||

SC 23Sen 1.87 kathaṁ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā asādhāraṇādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 24yaṁ nānyaḥ praṇidhiḥ kaścit* evaṁ satvasyopajāyate |
sarvasatvasukhārthāya tad asādhāraṇaṁ vidur iti ||

SC 25kathaṁ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā unnatādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 26aparatīrthikamataṁ śrutvāvajñā pratiṣṭhate |
unnatādhyāśayās tena narasiṁhā bhavanti te ti ||

SC 27kathaṁ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā akṛpaṇādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 28na kāmaguṇabhogārthaṁ saṁcinvanti śubhaṁ vidūḥ |
tataś cākṛpaṇās teṣāṁ bhavanty adhyāśayā sadeti ||

SC 29kathaṁ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā anivartiyādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 30kāmaiḥ te nāvakīryante buddhatve kṛtaniścayāḥ |
tenānivartiyās teṣām adhyāśayā iti smṛtāḥ ||

SC 31kathaṁ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā akṛtrimādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 32vaśi pratyekabuddhānāṁ na spṛhenti kathaṁcana |
evaṁ cākṛtrimo bhavati teṣām adhyāśayaḥ sadeti ||

SC 33kathaṁ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvāḥ śuddhādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 34Sen 1.88 lābhasatkāram utsṛjya paramārthābhikāṁkṣiṇo |
śuddha adhyāśayas teṣām ity evam upadiśyate ||

SC 35kathaṁ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā dṛḍhādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 36na saṁharanti vīriyaṁ dharme lokair abhidrutā |
evam adhyāśayās teṣāṁ dṛḍhā santi maharṣiṇām iti ||

SC 37kathaṁ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvāḥ svabhāvādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 38mūrchitās te na bhuṁjanti parānnāny avasrutā |
svabhāvādhyāśayas teṣām evam ārya praśaṁsyate ti ||

SC 39kathaṁ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvāḥ tṛptādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 40nātra prasyandanti kāmeṣu niṣkramyābhiratāḥ sadā |
evam adhyāśayo tṛpto bodhisatve praśaṁsyate ti ||

SC 41kathaṁ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvāḥ pudgalādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 42svayaṁbhūsarvadarśitvam abhikāṅkṣanti paṇḍitāḥ |
pudgalādhyāśayā bhavanti caivam apratimā dhruvāḥ ||

SC 43Sen 1.89 kathaṁ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā anantādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 44na prārthayanti mahābhogān adānaguṇasaṁpadā |
anantādhyāśayāś caivaṁ bhavanti puruṣottamā iti ||

SC 45sarvehi etehi viṁśadbhiḥ sarvadharmaviśāradā |
samanvitā satpuruṣā śubhair adhyāśayair varā iti ||

SC 46imehi khalu bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā viṁśadbhir adhyāśayaiḥ samanvāgatā bhavantīti ||

SC 47evam ukte āyuṣmān mahākāśyapa āyuṣmantaṁ mahākātyāyanam uvāca || katihi bho jinaputra ākārehi bodhisatvā dvitīyāyāṁ bhūmau vartamānās tṛtīyāyāṁ bhūmau vivartanti || evam ukte āyuṣmān mahākātyāyano āyuṣmantaṁ mahākāśyapam uvāca || aṣṭāviṁśadbhiḥ bho dhutadharmadhara kāraṇehi bodhisatvā dvitīyāyāṁ bhūmau vartamānāḥ tṛtīyāyāṁ bhūmau vivartante || katamair aṣṭāviṁśadbhir ākāraiḥ || tadyathā lābhagurukāś ca bhavanti | satkāragurukāś ca bhavanti | kīrtiślokaparāś ca bhavanti | śaṭhāś ca bhavanti | viṣameṇa ca vṛddhiṁ kalpayanti | gurukopanabhāṣaṇaparāś ca bhavanti | triṣu rataneṣu na ca citrikārabahulā vitaranti | dakṣiṇīyeṣu bodhisatvacaritaṁ ca na paryeṣanti | yataś ca bodhisatvacaritabhūmiṁ prāpnuvanti tāṁ na pūjayanti | atirekapūjāye prāpyaṁ ca bhāraṁ na upādiyanti | aprāpyaṁ ca bhāraṁ upādiyitvā vitaranti | ākīrṇavihāreṇa ca nārtīyanti | mālyavastrālaṁkārābhāraṇānulepanadharāś ca bhavanti | alpaguṇaparituṣṭāś Sen 1.90 ca bhavanti | abhīkṣṇaṁ lokaramaṇīyābhiratāś ca bhavanti | na ca sarvadhātū anityā saṁkalpayanti | svena ca varṇarūpeṇa param abhimanyanti | na ca viparītadarśanatyāgaṁ karonti | na ca yathoddiṣṭaṁ padavyaṁjanaṁ paripūrṇaṁ karonti | deśanāmatsariṇaś ca bhavanti | apātradarśinaś ca bhavanti | pātre ca na pratipādayanti | kaṭhinasantānāś ca bhavanti | asamīkṣakāriṇaś ca bhavanti || ye keci bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā dvitīyāyāṁ bhūmau vartamānā tṛtīyāyāṁ bhūmau vivartanti sarve te imehi-r-aṣṭāviṁśadbhir ākārair vivartanti ||

SC 48tatra idam iti ucyate ||

SC 49ity eṣā dvitīyā bhūmi bodhisatvānam ucyate |
nānākuśalakośānāṁ lokārthasukhacāriṇāṁ ||

SC 50ye hi doṣehi saṁyuktā vivartanti tathāvidhā |
ye caivaṁ parivartantā na vivartanti paṇḍitāḥ ||

SC 51durārohāṁ dhuradhīrāḥ pratipadyanti śūratāṁ |
tāṁ ca lokānukampārthaṁ bahuduḥkhā caranti te ||

SC 52te te devamanuṣyāṇāṁ pūjyāḥ sarve tathāgatāḥ |
tathā hi vividhaṁ duḥkhaṁ upenti jñānapūrvakaṁ ||

SC 53nānādhātum imaṁ lokam anuvartanti paṇḍitāḥ |
tena teṣāṁ gatā kīrti loke samarumānuṣe iti ||

iti śrīmahāvastu-avadāne dvitīyabhūmiḥ samāptā ||
