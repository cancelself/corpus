48. The Dreams of Śuddhodana and Others

SC 1atha śuddhodanaḥ svapnaṁ paśyati ||

SC 2paśyāmi putra supine taranāvagāḍhaṁ
abhyutthitaṁ gajavaraṁ maṇijālacchannaṁ ||

SC 3madhye purasya adhvanāvasthito ca rātrau
nirdhāvate puravarā abhikampamānaḥ ||

SC 4taṁ dṛṣṭvā mahya supine vipulaṁ ca hāsyaṁ
abhyutthitaṁ ruditam eva ca aprameyaṁ ||

SC 5kampe ca me saṁparitaptaṁ śarīram anta-
Sen 2.134 rdāhaṁ samuddhanati kiṁ tu bhaviṣyate dya ||

SC 6atha lokapāla avacū manujapradhānaṁ
mā bhāya bhūmipati saṁjanayāhi harṣaṁ ||

SC 7hanta śṛṇohi phalaṁ yaṁ supinasya tatvaṁ
samutthitaṁ bahujanasya vibodhanārthaṁ ||

SC 8eṣo mahāguṇadharo vijahitva rājyaṁ
dutiyās tathaiva caturā svajanaṁ ca sphītaṁ ||

SC 9niḥsaṁśayaṁ varabalaṁ anapekṣamāṇo
niryāsyate puravarā viditaṁ sa bhotu ||

SC 10etasmiṁ nirgate anekavidhaṁ ti duḥkhaṁ
jāyiṣyate supini yaṁ hasitaṁ ti tatvaṁ ||

SC 11yaṁ rodasi supini dāni sukhaṁ anantaṁ
śrutvā bhaviṣyati jitaṁ jitaśatrusaṁghaṁ ||

SC 12mātuḥsvasāpi supinaṁ paśyati ||

SC 13mātuḥsvasā avaca kāṁcanarāśivarṇa
paśyāmi putra supine ṛṣabhaṁ sujātaṁ ||

SC 14śvetaṁ sucārukakubhaṁ atiriktaśṛṁgaṁ
śṛṇgārasaṁsthitagatiṁ pratipūrṇadehaṁ ||

SC 15so garjati sumadhuraṁ kapilāhvayāto
nirdhāvate hṛdayadṛṣṭipathaṁ harantaḥ ||

SC 16na ca kaści taṁ prasahate abhigarjamānaṁ
pratigarjituṁ kumudarāśinibhaṁ sujātaṁ ||

SC 17Sen 2.135 te devarāja avacū karuṇaṁ rudantaṁ
mā roda śākyakulanandanajātarāga ||

SC 18vakṣyāmi te avitathāvacanaṁ vijālaṁ
ānandajāni upajānaya tvaṁ sukhāni ||

SC 19atyantaśuddhacaraṇaṁ kuśalopapetaṁ
eṣo nararṣabha gatīmatināṁ vidhijñaḥ ||

SC 20hitvā janaṁ puruṣasiṁhavaro puro ca
nirgamya te abhilaṣe puruṣarṣabhatvaṁ ||

SC 21so pi amṛtaṁ acalam acyutam aprakampyaṁ
nirvāṇam apratisamaṁ pratiśuddhacakṣuḥ ||

SC 22nirdiśya taṁ puruṣasiṁharutaṁ maharṣiḥ
yaṁ śrutva tīrthikagaṇā diśatāṁ vrajanti ||

SC 23yaśodharāpi supinaṁ paśyati ||

SC 24atha rāhulasya idam abravīt pi
maitrottareṇa madanena nibaddhacittā ||

SC 25deva śṛṇohi yatha adya mayāpi dṛṣṭaṁ
supinaṁ manoramaṁ phalaṁ ca me tad bhaveyāt* ||

SC 26śuddhodanasya kila rājakulaṁ narendra
megho samantaṁ kṣaṇena samādadanto ||

SC 27lokatrayaṁ prabalatoyadharo sughoṣo
vidyutpradīpa vipradyotayanto bahūni ||

SC 28Sen 2.136 so śītalaṁ vimalam apratimaṁ prasannaṁ
vāri pravṛṣya madhuraṁ abhigarjamāno ||

SC 29varṣeti sāgaradharo śayane nidāghaṁ
eṣo pi antaḥsukhito sa sahāṁpatīko ||

SC 30brahmā atha upagamitva idaṁ avoca
tāṁ rāhulasya jananīṁ śṛṇu mā viṣīda ||

SC 31iṣṭaṁ phalaṁ tava ayaṁ supino mahārtho
saṁpūryate capalam eva janehi prītiṁ ||

SC 32śuddhodanasya ayam ātmajo cārunetro
lokatrayaṁ jaladharo iva varṣamāṇo ||

SC 33pralhādayiṣyati mahāparitāpataptān*
dharmaṁ dhruvaṁ karuṇam apratimaṁ janetvā ||

SC 34bodhisatvo pi paṁca mahāsupināṁ paśyati | paramasaṁbodhiprāpto śrāvasthāṁ bhikṣūṇāṁ vyākaroti || tathāgato bhikṣavo pūrve saṁbodhim anabhisaṁbuddho paṁca mahāsupināni adrākṣīt* || tathāgatasya katamāni paṁca ||

SC 35tathāgatasya bhikṣavaḥ pūrve sambodhim anabhisaṁbuddhasya iyaṁ mahāpṛthivī uccaśayanamahāśayanam abhūṣi | sumeru parvatarājā bimbopadhānam abhūṣi | purastime mahāsamudre vāmā bāhā ohitā abhūṣi paścime ca mahāsamudre dakṣiṇā bāhā ohitā abhūṣi | dakṣiṇe pi mahāsamudre ubhau pādatalāni ohitāni abhūnsuḥ | tathāgato bhikṣavaḥ pūrve saṁbodhim anabhisaṁbuddho imaṁ prathamaṁ mahāsupinam adrākṣīt* ||

SC 36Sen 2.137 tathāgatasya bhikṣavaḥ pūrve saṁbodhim anabhisaṁbuddhasya kṣīrikā nāma tṛṇajāti nābhimaṇḍalād abhyugdamya yāvan nabham āsadya asthāsi || tathāgato bhikṣavaḥ pūrve saṁbodhim anabhisaṁbuddho imaṁ dvitīyaṁ mahāsupinam adrākṣīt* ||

SC 37tathāgatasya bhikṣavo pūrve saṁbodhim anabhisaṁbuddhasya lohitakā prāṇakā kālaśīrṣakā pādatalehi yāvaj jānumaṇḍalāni cchādayitvā asthānsuḥ | tathāgato bhikṣavaḥ pūrve sambodhim anabhisaṁbuddho imaṁ tṛtīyaṁ mahāsvapnam adrākṣīt* ||

SC 38tathāgatasya bhikṣavaḥ pūrve saṁbodhim anabhisaṁbuddhasya catvāri nānāvarṇāṁ anekavarṇā ca caturhi diśāhi vaihāyasaṁ gatvā tathāgatasya pādatalāni upajighritvā sarvaśvetā apavidhyinsuḥ | tathāgato bhikṣavaḥ pūrve saṁbodhim anabhisaṁbuddho imaṁ caturthaṁ mahāsupinam adrakṣīt* ||

SC 39tathāgato bhikṣavaḥ pūrve saṁbodhim anabhisaṁbuddho mahato mīḍhaparvatasya uparim anupalipyamāno caṁkramaṁ caṁkrame | tathāgato bhikṣavaḥ pūrve saṁbidhim anabhisaṁbuddho imaṁ paṁcamaṁ mahāsupinam adrākṣīt* ||
