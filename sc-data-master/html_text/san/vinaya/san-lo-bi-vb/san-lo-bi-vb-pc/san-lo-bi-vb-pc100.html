• pācattika-dharma 100

ūna-dvādaśa-varṣā gṛhi-caritā

Origin story

SC 1 Roth 214bhagavān śrāvastīyam viharati | tā dāni śākiya-kanyāyo malla-kanyāyo kolīya-kanyāyo gṛhi- caritāyo pravrajitāyo paṭu kāyo uccakṣukāyo kṣipra-niśāntikāyo | mahāprajāpatī gautamī bhagavantaṁ pṛcchati | labhyā dāni bhagavan ūna-viṁśati-varṣāṁ gṛhi-caritām upasthāpayituṁ | bhagavān āha | labhyā | tāyo aṣṭa-varṣām nava-varṣāṁ gṛhi-caritām upasthāpayanti | tā a-prati-saṁkhyāna-vartinyo bhavanti | yathā ūna-viṁśati-varṣāḥ | yāvad bhagavān āha | na kṣamati ūna-dvādaśa-varṣāṁ gṛhi-caritām upasthāpayituṁ | atha khalu bhagavān mahāprajāpatīṁ gautamīm āmantrayati | sannipātaya gautami bhikṣuṇīyo yāvat paryavadātāni bhaviṣyanti |

Ruling

SC 2 yā puna bhikṣuṇī ūna-dvādaśa-varṣāṁ gṛhi-caritām upasthāpayet pācattikaṁ |

Definitions

  • gṛhi-caritā ti gṛhi-caritā ti vikopita-brahmacaryā |

Permutations

SC 3 yā eṣā gṛhi-caritā ākāṁkṣati tathāgata-pravedite dharma-vinaye upasaṁpadaṁ bhikṣuṇī-bhāvaṁ sā tāya anugrāhitavyā | peyālaṁ | yāvat hasta-pādā nivāsayitavyāḥ | tena bhagavān āha |
