source: Ernst Waldschmidt: Bruchstücke des Bhikṣuṇī-Prātimokṣa der Sarvāstivādins, mit einer Darstellung der Überlieferung des Bhikṣuṇī-Prātimokṣa in den verschiedenen Schulen, Leipzig, 1926.

Manuscript found during the German Turfan expeditions.

Waldschmidt ID: Pb; alternative ID: Kat.-Nr. 539, refering to the "Sanskrithandschriften aus den Turfanfunden" (SHT) catalogue of the German Turfan expeditions. Now available at the international dunhuang project website, idp.bl.uk. The database includes (copyrighted) photos of Pb3 = 539c and its 3 broken off pieces Kat.-Nr. 3215, Kat.-Nr. 3216 and Kat.-Nr. 3500

Pb1 and Pb2 were reported missing during WW2, thus there are no photos available.

find spot: Sāngim

explanation of abbreviations: Manuscript IDs: manuscript consists of 3 paper snippets, numbered Pb1, Pb2, Pb3 - each with text on recto and verso, marked with r and v, thus p. ex. Pb1r.

⊙ : string hole
