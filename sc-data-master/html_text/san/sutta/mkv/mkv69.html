• Mahākarmavibhaṅga

69

SC 1katame daśānuśaṁsā bhojanapradānasya. ucyate. balavān bhavati. varṇavān bhavati. sukhito bhavati. pratibhānavān bhavati. dīrghāyur bhavati. mahājanābhigamyo bhavati. priyadarśano bhavati. mahābhogaś ca bhavati. svargeṣūpapadyate. kṣipraṁ ca parinirvāti.

SC 2a) yathoktaṁ bhagavatā vaiśālyāṁ siṁhasya rājaputrasya. dadan siṁha priyo bhavati. santo janā bhajanti. kalyāṇaśabdo bhavati. viśāradaḥ parṣadam avagāhati. kālagato deveṣūpapadyate.

SC 3gāthā coktā.

SC 4Mkv 93te tatra nandanti ramanti ca modayanti
samarpitāḥ kāmaguṇeṣu pañcasu
kṛtvā hi vākyam amitasya tāyino
modanti te sugatavarasya śrāvakāḥ.

SC 5etad dānasya phalaṁ bhagavatoktam.

SC 6svargeṣūpapadyate kṣipraṁ ca parinirvāti. āha ca.

SC 7nānārasavyañjanasupraṇītaṁ
kāle śubhaṁ gandharasopapannam
dadāti yaḥ pātram avekṣya dānaṁ
sadāryasaṁghasya guṇān vicintya
āyuś ca varṇaṁ ca yaśo balaṁ ca
saṁprāpya bhogān pratibhāṁ sukhaṁ ca
divīha caiva prathitottamaśrīḥ
sukhāt sukhaṁ yāti vimokṣam ante.

SC 8ime daśa guṇā bhojanapradānasya.
