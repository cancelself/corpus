Agrajñasūtra

Wal 1 bhavati sa samayo yad ayaṁ lokaḥ saṁvartate | saṁvartamāne loke yadbhūyasā satvā ābhāsvare devanikāye upapadyante | te tatra bhavanti rūpiṇo manomayā avikalā ahīnendriyāḥ sarvāṅgapratyaṁ gopetāḥ śubhā varṇasthāyinaḥ svayaṁprabhā vaihāyasagāminaḥ prītibhakṣāḥ prītyāhārā dīrghāyuṣo dīrgham adhvānaṁ tiṣṭhanti |

Wal 2 tena khalu samayena mahāpṛthivī ekodikā bhavati ekārṇavā | tasyāṁ ekodikāyāṁ mahāpṛthivyāṁ ekārṇavāyāṁ vāyunā śaraḥ saṁmūrcchitaḥ saṁtanoti tadyathā pakvasya kṣīrasya śaraḥ saṁmūrcchitaḥ saṁtanoty evam eva tasyāṁ ekodikāyāṁ mahāpṛthivyāṁ ekārṇavāyāṁ vayunā śaraḥ saṁmūrcchitaḥ saṁtanoti | sa bhavati pṛthivīraso varṇasaṁpanno gandhasaṁpanno rasasaṁpannaḥ | tadyathā navanītam evaṁvarṇaḥ | tadyathā kṣaudraṁ madhv aneḍakam evamāsvādaḥ |

Wal 3 bhavati sa samayo yad ayaṁ loko vivartate | vivartamāne loke satvā āyuḥkṣayāt karmakṣayāt puṇyakṣayād ābhāsvarād devanikāyāc cavitvā ihatye manuṣyasabhāgatām āgaccanti | te ’pi rūpiṇo pūrvavad yāvad dīrgham adhvānam tiṣṭhanti |

Wal 4 tena khalu samayena na sūryacandramasau prajñāyete na rātridivasaṁ na māsaṁ ardhamāsaṁ na ṛtuṁ na kṣaṇalavamuhūrtaṁ prajñāyante … na strī na pumān | satvaḥ satva ity ekasaṁkhyāṁ gacchati |

Wal 5 athānyatamo lolupajātīyaḥ satvaḥ aṁgulyagreṇa pṛthivīrasam āsvādayati | yathā yathāsvādayati tathā tathā pratikāṁkṣati | yathā yathā pratikāṁkṣati tathā tathā kabaḍiṁkāropakrameṇa praparibhuktaḥ |

Wal 6 adrākṣur anyatame satvās taṁ satvaṁ kabaḍiṅkāropakrameṇa pṛthivīrasaṁ paribhuñjānam | dṛṣṭvā ca te ’pi satvā aṁgulyāgreṇa pṛthivīrasam āsvādayanti | yathā yathāsvādayanti pūrvavad yāvad kabaḍiṅkāropakrameṇa praparibhuktāḥ | yataś ca te satvā taṁ pṛthivīrasaṁ kabaḍiṅkāropakrameṇa praparibhuktās teṣāṁ kāye dṛḍhatvaṁ gurutvaṁ prajñāyete śubhavarṇo ’ntarhitaḥ | andhakāro loke prādurbhūtaḥ |

Wal 7 dharmatā khalu vāsiṣṭha andhakārasya loke prādurbhāvāt sūryacandramasau prajñāyete etc. as in 4.

Waldschmidt, Ernst 1970b. Fragment of a Buddhist Sanskrit text on cosmogony. In Tilakasiri, J. (ed.), Añjali. Papers on Indology and Buddhism. O. H. Vijesekera felicitation volume: 40–45. Peradeniya. Reprinted in AKS, 290–295.
