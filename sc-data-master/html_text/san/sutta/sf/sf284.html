Veṇukātyāyanasagotrīsūtra

Wal 1 pṛccha bhagini śrutvā te vedayiṣyāmi |

Wal 2 saṁti śramaṇa eke śramaṇa brāhmaṇāḥ ye svayaṁkṛtaṁ sukhaduḥkhaṁ prajñapayaṁti | saṁty eke parakṛtaṁ prajñapayaṁti saṁty eke svayaṁkṛtaṁ ca parakṛtaṁ ca | saṁty eke ’svayaṁkāra?aparakārahetusamutpannaṁ sukhaduḥkhaṁ prajñapayaṁti | yathā kathaṁ samutpannam arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkhaṁ prajñapayaṁti |

Wal 3 anyathāsamutpannam arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkhaṁ prajñapayaṁti |

Wal 4 yathā kathaṁ śramaṇa anyathāsamutpannam arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkhaṁ prajñapayaṁti |

Wal 5 pratītyasamutpannaṁ bhagini arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkhaṁ prajñapayaṁti |

Wal 6 yathā kathaṁ śramaṇa pratītyasamutpannaṁ arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkhaṁ prajñpayaṁti |

Wal 7 tena hi bhagini tvām eva prakṣyāmi yathā te kṣamati tathaitaṁ vyākuru |

Wal 8 kiṁ manyase bhagini | asti cakṣuḥ | asti śramaṇa | asti rūpam | asti śramaṇa |

Wal 9 cakṣuś ced bhagini utpadyeta api nu asti cakṣuḥsaṁsparśapratyayam ādhyātmam utpadyate vedayitaṁ sukhaṁ vā duḥkhaṁ vā aduḥkhāsukhaṁ vā | asti śramaṇa |

Wal 10 asti śrotram asti ghāṇam asti jihvā asti kāyaḥ asti manaḥ |

asti śramaṇa | saṁti dharmāḥ |

Wal 11 asti śrotraṁ ghrāṇaṁ jihvā kāyo manaś ced utpadyeta api nu śrotraghrāṇajihvākāyamanaḥsaṁsparśapratyayam ādhyātmam utpadyate vedayitaṁ sukhaṁ vā duḥkhaṁ vā aduḥkhāsukhaṁ vā |

asti śramaṇa |

Wal 12 evaṁ hi bhagini pratīyasamutpannam arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkhaṁ prajñapayaṁti |

Wal 13 evaṁ hi śramaṇa pratītyasamutpannam arhantaḥ sukhaṁ prajñapya yathā kathaṁ śramaṇa arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkhasya nirodhaṁ prajñapayaṁti |

Wal 14 tena hi bhagini tvām eva prakṣyāmi yathā te kṣamati tathaitaṁ vyākuru |

Wal 15 kiṁ manyase bhagini | cakṣuś ced bhagini sarveṇa sarvaṁ sarvathā apariśeṣaṁ nirudhyeta apariśeṣan adhyastaṁ parikṣayam paryādānaṁ gacched api nu cakṣuḥsaṁsparśapratyayam ādhyātmam utpadyate vedayitaṁ sukhaṁ vā duḥkhaṁ vā aduḥkhāsukhaṁ vā |

no śramaṇa |

Wal 16 śrotraṁ ghrāṇaṁ jihvā kāyo manaś ced bhagini sarveṇa sarvaṁ sarvathā apariśeṣaṁ nirudhyeta apariśeṣam adhyastaṁ parikṣayaṁ paryādānaṁ gacchet api nu manaḥsaṁsparśapratyayam ādhyātmam utpadyate vedayitaṁ sukhaṁ vā duḥkhaṁ vā aduḥkhāsukhaṁ vā |

no śramaṇa |

Wal 17 evaṁ hi bhagini ārhantaḥ pratītyasamutpannasya sukhaduḥkhasya nirodhaṁ prajñapayaṁti |

Wal 18 asmiṁ khalu dharmaparyāye bhāṣyamāne veṇukātyāyanasagotryā virajo vigatamalaṁ dharmeṣu dharmacakṣur utpannam …

Wal 19 atha veṇukātyāyanasagotrī dṛṣṭadharmā prāptadharmā viditadharmā paryavagāḍhadharmā tīrṇakāṁkṣā tīrṇavicikitsā aparapratyayā ananyaneyā śāstuḥ śāsane dharmeṣu vaiśaradyaprāptā utthāyāsanād ekāṁsam uttarāsaṁgaṁ kṛtvā yenāyuṣmān udāyī tenāṁjaliṁ praṇamya āyuṣmantam udāyinam idam avocat |

Wal 20 abhikrāntāhaṁ śramaṇa abhikrāntā eṣāhaṁ bhagavantaṁ śaraṇaṁ gacchāmi dharmaṁ ca bhikṣusaṁghaṁ ca | upāsikāṁ ca mān dhāraya śramaṇa adyāgreṇa yāvajjīvaṁ prāṇopetaṁ śaraṇaṁgatām abhiprasannām |

Wal 21 atha udāyī veṇukātyāyanasagotrīṁ brāhmaṇīṁ dhārmyā kathayāsaṁdarśayitvā samādapayitvā samuttejayitvā saṁpraharṣayitvā utthāyāsanāt prakrāntaḥ |

Waldschmidt, Ernst 1980c. On a Sanskrit version of the Verahaccāni Sutta of the Saṁyuttanikāya. NAWG 1980/4: 69–76. Reprinted in AKS, 362–369.
