Satyasūtra

Wal 1 śrāvastyāṁ nidānam |

Wal 2 ye kecid bhikṣavaḥ samyag eva śraddhayā agārād anagārikāṁ pravrajaṁti sarve te caturṇāṁ āryasatyānāṁ jñānāya |

Wal 3 katameṣāṁ caturṇām |

Wal 4 duḥkham āryasatyasya duḥkhasamudayasya duḥkhanirodhasya duḥkhanirodhagāminī pratipad āryasatyasya jñānāya |

Wal 5 tasmāt tarhi caturṇām āryasatyānām anabhisamitānām abhisamayāya atimātraṁ tīvracchando vyāyāmaś cotsudhiś cāprativāṇiś ca smṛtyā saṁprajanyena ca yogaḥ karaṇīyaḥ |

Wal 6 katameṣāṁ caturṇām |

Wal 7 duḥkham āryasatyasya duḥkhasamudayasya duḥkhanirodhagāminī pratipad āryasatyasya iti |

Wal 8 … o … ai … r … satya sūtraṁ samāptaḥ |

Wal 9 rakṣaṁ bhavatu || … syasya svāhā ||
