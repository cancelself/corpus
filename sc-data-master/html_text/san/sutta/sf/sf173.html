Bhūtamidaṁsūtra

Tri 24.1 tatra bhagavān āyuṣmantaṁ śāriputram āmantrayati |

Tri 24.2a uktam idaṁ śāriputra mayā parāyaṇeṣv ajitapraśneṣu |

ye ca saṅkhyātadharmāṇi ye ca śaikṣāḥ pṛthagvidhāḥ | teṣāṁ me nipakasyeryāṁ pṛṣṭaḥ prabrūhi māriṣa || ||

Tri 24.2b ke śāriputra śaikṣāḥ ke ca saṅkhyātadharmāṇi |

Tri 24.3 evam ukta āyuṣmāñ chāriputras tūṣṇī |

Tri 24.4 dvir api trir api bhagavān āyuṣmantaṁ śāriputram idam avocat |

Tri 24.5 uktam idaṁ śāriputra mayā parāyaṇesv ajitapraśneṣu |

ye ca saṅkhyātadharmāḥ pūrvavat |

Tri 24.6 dvir api trir apy āyuṣmāñ chāriputras tūṣṇīm abhūt |

Tri 24.7 tatra bhagavān āyuṣmantaṁ śāriputram āmantrayati |

Tri 24.8 bhūtam idaṁ śāriputra |

Tri 24.9 evam etad bhūtaṁ bhadanta | bhūtasya bhadanta bhikṣur nirvide virāgāya nirodhāya pratipanno bhavati |

Tri 24.10 tadāhārasaṁbhavaḥ | tadāhārasaṁbhavasya bhikṣur nirvide virāgāya nirodhāya pratipanno bhavati |

Tri 24.11 tadāhāranirodha iti | yad bhūtaṁ tan nirodhadharmam iti viditvā nirodhadharmasya bhikṣur nirvide virāgāya nirodhāya pratipanno bhavati |

Tri 24.12 ima ucyante śaikṣāḥ |

Tri 24.13 bhūtam idaṁ śāriputra |

Tri 24.14 evam etad bhūtaṁ bhadanta | bhūtasya bhadanta bhikṣur nirvide virāgāya nirodhāyānupādāyāsravebhyaḥ samyaksuvimuktacitto bhavati |

Tri 24.15 tadāhārasaṁbhavaḥ | tadāhārasaṁbhavasya bhikṣur nirvide virāgāya nirodhāyānupādāyāsravebhyaḥ samyaksuvimuktacitto bhavati |

Tri 24.16 tadāhāranirodha iti | yad bhūtaṁ tan nirodhadharmam iti viditvā nirodhadharmasya bhikṣur nirvide virāgāya nirodhāyānupādāyāsravebhyaḥ samyaksuvimuktacitto bhavati |

Tri 24.17 ima ucyante saṅkhyātadharmāṇaḥ |

Tri 24.18 evam etac cchāriputra bhūtam | evam etac cchāriputra bhūtasya bhikṣur nirvide virāgāya pūrvavad yāvat saṅkhyātadharmāṇaḥ |

Tri 24.19 atha bhagavān utthāyāsanād vihāraṁ prāviśat pratisaṁlayanāya |

Tri 24.20 athāyuṣmāñ chāriputro ’ciraprakrāntaṁ bhagavantaṁ viditvā bhikṣūn āmantrayati |

Tri 24.21 apratisaṁviditaṁ mām āyuṣmanto bhagavān etaṁ prathamaṁ praśnaṁ pṛṣṭavān | tasya me ’bhūd apūrvaṁ dhandhāyitatvam |

Tri 24.22 yataś ca me bhagavāṁs tat prathamaṁ praśnavyākaraṇam abhyanumoditavān tasya me etad abhavat |

Tri 24.23 sacet kevalikāṁ rātriṁ bhagavān etam evārtham anyaiḥ padair anyair vyañjanaiḥ praśnaṁ pṛcchet kevalikām apy ahaṁ rātriṁ bhagavata etam evārtham anyaiḥ padair anyair vyañjanaiḥ praśnaṁ pṛṣṭo vyākuryām |

Tri 24.24 saced ekam divasam | ekaṁ rātridivasam | saptāpi rātridivasāni bhagavān mām etam evārtham anyaiḥ padair anyair vyañjanaiḥ praśnaṁ pṛcchet | saptāpy ahaṁ rātridivasāni bhagavata etam evārtham anyaiḥ padair anyair vyañjanaiḥ praśnaṁ pṛṣṭo vyākuryām |

Tri 24.25 athānyataro bhikṣur yena bhagavāṁs tenopajagāma | upetya bhagavatpādau śirasā vanditvaikānte ’sthāt | ekāntasthitaḥ sa bhikṣur bhagavantam idam avocat |

Tri 24.26 āyuṣmatā bhadanta śāriputreṇa udārārṣahī vāg bhāṣitaikāṁśa udgṛhītaḥ pariṣadi samyaksiṁhanādo nāditaḥ |

Tri 24.27 apratisaṁviditaṁ mām āyuṣmanto bhagavān etaṁ pūrvavat |

Tri 24.28 sacet kevalikām apy ahaṁ rātrim āyuṣmantaṁ śāriputram etam evārtham anyaiḥ padair anyair vyañjanaiḥ praśnaṁ pṛccheyaṁ yāvat saptāpi rātridivasāni śāriputra etam evārtham anyaiḥ padair anyair vyañjanaiḥ praśnaṁ pṛṣṭo vyākuryāt |

Tri 24.29 tathā hi śāriputrasya bhikṣor dharmadhātuḥ supratividdhaḥ |
