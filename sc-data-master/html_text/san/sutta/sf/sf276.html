Candrasūtra

Wal 1 evaṁ mayā śrutam | ekasamayaṁ bhagavāṁ caṁpāyāṁ viharati gargarapuṣkariṇyās tīre |

Wal 2 tena khalu samayena rāhuṇā asurendreṇa sarvaṁ candramaṇḍalam āvṛtaṁ |

Wal 3 atha yā devatā tasmiṁ candramaṇḍale sthitā bhītā trastā saṁvignā hṛṣṭaromakūpā yena bhagavāṁs tenopajagāma | upetya bhagavatpādau śirasā vanditvaikānte ’sthāt | ekāntasthitā bhagavaṁtaṁ gāthayā babhāṣe |

Wal 4 namas te buddha vīrāstu vipramuktāya savataḥ |
saṁbādhapratipanno ’smi tasya me śaraṇaṁ bhava ||

Wal 5 loke sugatam arhantaṁ candramāḥ śaraṇaṁ gataḥ |
rāhoś candramasaṁ muñca buddhā lokānukaṁpakāḥ ||

Wal 6 bhagavāṁ gāthayā pratyābhāṣīt |

yo hy andhakāre nabhasi
prabhākaro vairocano … |

rāho śaśāṅkaṁ grasa māntarīkṣe
prajāpradīpaṁ tvaritaṁ vimuñca ||

Wal 7 … | saṁsvinnagātro vyathitaḥ saṁbādhito yathā naraḥ | saṁtvaramāṇo ’sau rāhuś candramaṇḍalam utsṛṣṭaḥ ||

Wal 8 tena samayena baḍir virocanapautra āsīd asuraḥ | adrākṣīd baḍir virocanapautro rāhuṇā asurendreṇa tvaritatvaritaṁ candramaṇḍalam utsṛṣṭam dṛṣṭvā ca tasyāṁ velāyāṁ gāthāṁ babhāṣe ||

Wal 9 kiṁ nu saṁtvaramāṇas tvaṁ rāhuś candraṁ vimuñcasi | saṁsvinnagātro vyathitaḥ saṁbādhito yathā naraḥ ||

Wal 10 rāhur avocat || saptadhā me sphalen mūrdhā jīvan na sukham āpnuyāṁ tatra buddhābhigītena muñceyaṁ śaśinaṁ na cet ||

Wal 11 baḍir virocanapautra gāthayā babhāṣe ||

… madarśinaḥ saṁbuddhāḥ … |
teṣāṁ gāthābhigītena rāhuś candraṁ vimuñcati ||

Wal 12 candrasūtraṁ samāptam ||

Waldschmidt, Ernst 1970a. Buddha frees the disc of the moon (Candrasūtra). Bulletin of the School of Oriental and African Studies 33: 179–183. Reprinted in AKS, 296–301.
