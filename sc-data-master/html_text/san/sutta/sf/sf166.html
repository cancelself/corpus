Bhikṣusūtra

Tri 17.1 anyataro bhikṣur yena bhagavāṁs tenopajagāma | upetya bhagavatpādau śirasā vanditvaikānte ’sthāt | ekāntasthitaḥ sa bhikṣur bhagavantam idam avocat |

Tri 17.2 kin nu bhagavatā pratītyasamutpādaḥ kṛta aho svid anyaiḥ |

Tri 17.3 na bhikṣo mayā pratītyasamutpādaḥ kṛto nāpy anyaiḥ |

Tri 17.4 api tūtpādād vā tathāgatānām anutpādād vā sthitā eveyaṁ dharmatā dharmasthitaye dhātuḥ | taṁ tathāgataḥ svayam abhijñāyābhisaṁbuddhyākhyāti prajñapayati prasthāpayati vibhajati vivaraty uttānīkaroti deśayati saṁprakāśayati |

Tri 17.5 yadutāsmin satīdaṁ bhavaty asyotpādād idam utpadyate | yadutāvidyāpratyayāḥ saṁskārā yāvat samudayo nirodhaś ca bhavati ||
