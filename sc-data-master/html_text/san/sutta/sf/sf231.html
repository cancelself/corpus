Ekottarikāgama 29

Tri 29.1 dvau dharmau śaikṣasya bahukarau bhavataḥ |

Tri 29.101 hrīr vyapatrāpyaṁ ca | evam suvacatvaṁ kalyāṇamitratā ca | āpattikauśalyam āpattivyutthānakauśalyaṁ ca | samāpattikauśalyam samāpattivyutthānakauśalyaṁ ca | dhātukauśalyaṁ manasikārakauśalyaṁ ca | ārjavatā mārdavaṁ ca | kṣāntiḥ sauratyaṁ ca | sākhilyaṁ ca pratisaṁstaraś ca | smṛtiś ca saṁprajanyaṁ ca | pratisaṁkhyānabalaṁ bhāvanābalaṁ ca | indriyair guptadvāratā bhojane mātrajñatā ca | śīlasaṁpattir dṛṣṭisaṁpattiś ca | śīlaviśuddhir dṛṣṭiviśuddhiś ca | dṛṣṭiviśuddhis tathādṛṣṭeś ca yoniśaḥ pradhānam | saṁvejanīyeṣu dharmeṣu saṁvegaḥ tathāsaṁvignasya ca yoniśaḥ pradhānaṁ | asaṁtuṣṭiḥ kuśaleṣu dharmeṣu aprativāṇiś ca prahāṇe | śamathaś ca vipaśyanā ca | vidyā ca vimuktiś ca | |

Tri 29.2 dve jñāne śaikṣasya bahukare bhavataḥ | dharmajñānam anvayajñānaṁ ca | evaṁ

paracittajñānaṁ saṁvṛtijñānaṁ ca | duḥkhajñānaṁ samudayajñānaṁ ca | nirodhajñānaṁ mārgajñānaṁ ca | duḥkhe dharmajñānaṁ duḥkhe ’nvayajñānaṁ ca | nirodhe dharmajñānaṁ nirodhe ’nvayajñānaṁ ca | mārge dharmajñānaṁ mārge ’nvayajñānaṁ ca | |

Tri 29.3 dvau hetū dvau pratyayau śaikṣasya samyagdṛṣṭer utpādāya |
parataś ca ghoṣa adhyātmaṁ ca yoniśo manasikāraḥ | |

Tri 29.4 dve satye śaikṣasya bahukare bhavataḥ |
saṁvṛtisatyaṁ paramārthasatyaṁ ca | |

Tri 29.5 dvayor dharmayoḥ śaikṣeṇa tīvraś cetasaḥ ārakṣāsmṛtyapramāde yogaḥ karaṇīyaḥ | satye cākopye ca | |

Tri 29.6 dvau dharmau śaikṣasya bahukarau bhavataḥ | upasthitasmṛtitā ca kāye notkṣiptacakṣuṣkatā ca antargṛhe |

Tri 29.7 dvau dharmau śaikṣasya bahukarau bhavataḥ |
/// anagāre cābhiratiḥ | |

Tri 29.81 dvau dharmau śaikṣasya parihāṇāya saṁvartate |
labhenānunīyate alābhena pratihanyate |

Tri 29.82 dvau dharmau śaikṣasya aparihāṇāya saṁvartate |
labhena nānunīyate alābhena na pratihanyate |

yathā lābha alābha evaṁ yaśa ayaśo nindā praśaṁsā sukhaṁ duḥkhaṁ | |

Tri 29.91 dvau dharmau śaikṣasya rāgaparihāṇāya saṁvartate |
śamathaś ca vipaśyanā ca |

Tri 29.92 dvau dharmau śaikṣasya lobhaparihāṇāya saṁvartate |
śamathaś ca vipaśyanā ca |

yathā lobha evaṁ dveṣo mohaḥ pūrvavad yathā likhitaṁ |

Tri 2_29.101 dvau dharmau śaikṣasya prajñāpratilaṁbhāya saṁvartate |
śamathaś ca vipaśyanā ca |

Tri 29.102 yathā prajñāpratilaṁbhāya evaṁ pūrvavad yathoktaḥ prajñāmārgaḥ | | | |
