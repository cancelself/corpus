• Arthaviniścayasūtram

7. Dvāviṁśatir-indriyāṇi

Tatra bhikṣavaḥ katamāni dvā-viṁśatir-indriyāṇi?

Yad-uta:

  1. Cakṣur-indriyam |
  2. śrotrendriyam |
  3. ghrāṇendriyam |
  4. jihvendriyam |
  5. kāyendriyam |
  6. mana-indriyam |
  7. strī-indriyam |
  8. puruṣendriyam |
  9. jīvitendriyam |
  10. duḥkhendriyam |
  11. daurmanasyendriyam |
  12. sukhendriyam |
  13. saumanasyendriyam |
  14. upekṣendriyam |
  15. śraddhendriyam |
  16. vīryendriyam |
  17. smṛtīndriyam |
  18. samādhīndriyam |
  19. prajñendriyam |
  20. anājñātam-ājñāsyāmīndriyam |
  21. ājñendriyam |
  22. ājñātāvīndriyam ||

Imāni bhikṣavo dvā-viṁśatir-indriyāṇi ||
