13. Catvāri Smṛty-Upasthānāni

Tatra bhikṣavaḥ katamāni catvāri smṛty-upasthānāni?

  1. Iha bhikṣavo bhikṣur-adhyātmaṁ kāye kāyānudarśī viharati | ātāpī saṁprajānan smṛtimān vinīya loke abhidhyā-daurmanasye | bahirdhā kāye kāyānudarśī viharati | adhyātma-bahirdhā kāye kāyānudarśī viharati | ātāpī saṁprajānan smṛtimān, vinīya loke abhidhyā-daurmanasye ||
  2. Adhyātma-vedanāsu bahirdhā-vedanāsu adhyātma-bahirdhā | vedanāsu vedanānudarśī viharati | ātāpī saṁprajānan smṛtimān, vinīya loke abhidhyā-daurmanasye ||
  3. Adhyātma-citte bahirdhā-citte adhyātma-bahirdhā citte cittānudarśī viharati | ātāpī saṁprajānan smṛtimān, vinīya loke abhidhyā-daurmanasye ||
  4. Adhyātma-dharmeṣu bahirdhā-dharmeṣu adhyātma-bahirdhā dharmeṣu dharmānudarśī viharati | ātāpī saṁprajānan smṛtimān, vinīya loke abhidhyā-daurmanasye ||

Imāni bhikṣavaś-catvāri smṛty-upasthānāni ||
