23: saṁstavaparivartastrayoviṁśaḥ

SC 1Vai 259atha khalu śuddhāvāsakāyikā devaputrā bodhimaṇḍaniṣaṇṇaṁ tathāgataṁ pradakṣiṇīkṛtya divyaiścandanacūrṇavarṣairabhyavakīrya ābhiḥ sārūpyābhirgāthābhirabhituṣṭuvuḥ—

SC 2utpanno lokapradyoto lokanāthaḥ prabhaṁkaraḥ |
andhabhūtasya lokasya cakṣurdātā raṇaṁjahaḥ || Verse 23.1

SC 3bhavān vijitasaṁgrāmaḥ puṇyaiḥ pūrṇamanorathaḥ |
saṁpūrṇaḥ śukladharmaiśca jagattvaṁ tarpayiṣyasi || Verse 23.2

SC 4uttīrṇapaṅko hyanighaḥ sthale tiṣṭhati gautamaḥ |
anyāṁ sattvāṁ mahoghena prodgatastārayiṣyasi || Verse 23.3

SC 5udgatastvaṁ mahāprājño lokeṣvapratipudgalaḥ |
lokadharmairaliptastvaṁ jalasthamiva paṅkajaḥ || Verse 23.4

SC 6ciraprasuptamimaṁ lokaṁ tamaskandhāvaguṇṭhitam |
bhavān prajñāpradīpena samarthaḥ pratibodhitum || Verse 23.5

SC 7cirāture jīvaloke kleśavyādhiprapīḍite |
vaidyarāṭ tvaṁ samutpannaḥ sarvavyādhipramocakaḥ || Verse 23.6

SC 8bhaviṣyantyakṣaṇā śūnyā tvayi nāthe samudgate |
manuṣyāścaiva devāśca bhaviṣyanti sukhānvitāḥ || Verse 23.7

SC 9yeṣāṁ tvaddarśanaṁ saumya eṣyase puruṣarṣabha |
na te kalpasahasrāṇi jātu yāsyanti durgatim || Verse 23.8

SC 10paṇḍitāścāpyarogāśca dharmaṁ śroṣyanti ye ’pi te |
gambhīrāścopadhīkṣīṇā bhaviṣyanti viśāradāḥ || Verse 23.9

SC 11mokṣyante ca laghuṁ sarve chittvā vai kleśabandhanam |
yāsyanti nirupādānāḥ phalaprāptivaraṁ śubham || Verse 23.10

SC 12dakṣiṇīyāśca te loke āhutīnāṁ pratigrahāḥ |
na teṣu dakṣiṇā nyūnā sattvānirvāṇahetukī || Verse 23.11

SC 13evaṁ khalu bhikṣavaḥ śuddhāvāsakāyikā devaputrāstathāgatamabhiṣṭutyaikānte prāñjalayastasthuḥ, prāñjalayastathāgataṁ namasyantaḥ ||

SC 14atha khalu cābhāsvarā devaputrāstathāgataṁ bodhimaṇḍaniṣaṇṇaṁ divyairnānāprakāraiḥ puṣpadhūpagandhamālyavilepanachatradhvajapatākābhiḥ saṁpūjya tripradakṣiṇīkṛtya cābhirgāthābhirabhyastāviṣuḥ— Vai 260

SC 15gambhīrabuddhe madhurasvarā mune brahmasvarā munivaragīta susvaram |
varāgrabodhi paramārthaprāptā sarvasvare pāragate namaste || Verse 23.12

SC 16trātāsi dīpo ’si parāyaṇo ’si nātho ’si loke kṛpamaitracittaḥ |
vaidyottamastvaṁ khalu śalyahartā cikitsakastvaṁ paramaṁ hitaṁkaraḥ || Verse 23.13

SC 17dīpaṁkarasya sahadarśanaṁ tvayā samudānitaṁ maitrakṛpābhrajālam |
pramuñca nāthā amṛtasya dhārāṁ śamehi tāpaṁ suramānuṣāṇām || Verse 23.14

SC 18tvaṁ padmabhūtaṁ tribhaveṣvaliptaṁ tvaṁ merukalpo vicalo hyakampyaḥ |
tvaṁ vajrakalpo hyacalapratijña tvaṁ candramā sarvaguṇāgradhārī || Verse 23.15

SC 19evaṁ khalu bhikṣava ābhāsvarā devāstathāgatamabhisaṁstutyaikānte tasthuḥ prāñjalayastathāgataṁ namasyantaḥ ||

SC 20atha khalu subrahmadevaputrapramukhā brahmakāyikā devāstathāgataṁ bodhimaṇḍaniṣaṇṇamanekamaṇiratnakoṭīnayutaśatasahasrapratyuptena ratnajālenābhichādya tripradakṣiṇīkṛtya cābhiḥ sārūpyābhirgāthābhirabhyastāviṣuḥ—

SC 21śubhavimalaprajña prabhatejadharā dbātriṁśallakṣaṇavarāgradharā |
smṛtimaṁ matimaṁ guṇajñānadharā akilāntakā śirasi vandami te || Verse 23.16

SC 22amalā vimalā trimalairvimalā trailokyaviśruta trividyagatā |
trividhāvimokṣavaracakṣudadā vandāmi tvāṁ trinayanaṁ vimalam || Verse 23.17

SC 23Vai 261kalikaluṣa uddhṛta sudāntamanā kṛpakaruṇa udgata jagārthakarā |
muni mudita udgata praśāntamanā dvayamativimocaka upekṣaratā || Verse 23.18

SC 24vrata tapasa udgata jagārthakarā | svacarīviśuddhacaripāragatā |
catusatyadarśaka vimokṣaratā mukto vimocayasi cānyajagat || Verse 23.19

SC 25balavīrya āgatu ihā namuci prajñāya vīrya tava maitrya jito |
prāptaṁ ca te padavaraṁ amṛtaṁ vandāma te śaṭhacamūmathanā || Verse 23.20

SC 26evaṁ khalu bhikṣavaḥ subrahmadevaputrapramukhā brahmakāyikā devāstathāgatamābhirgāthābhirabhiṣṭutya ekānte tasthuḥ prāñjalayastathāgataṁ namasyantaḥ ||

SC 27atha khalu te śuklapākṣikā māraputrā yena tathāgatastenopasaṁkrāman | upasaṁkramya mahāratnachatravitānaistathāgatamabhicchādya prāñjalayastathāgatamābhiḥ sārūpyābhirgāthābhirabhyastāviṣuḥ—

SC 28pratyakṣe ’smi bale tavātivipule mārasya ghorā camū
yatsā māracamū mahāpratibhayā ekakṣaṇe te jitā |
na ca te utthitu naiva kāyu trasito no vā girā vyāhṛtā
tvāṁ vandāmahi sarvalokamahitaṁ sarvārthasiddhaṁ munim || Verse 23.21

SC 29mārā koṭisahasranekanayutā gaṅgāṇubhiḥ saṁmitāḥ
te tubhyaṁ na samartha bodhisuvaṭā saṁcālituṁ kampitum |
yajñā koṭisahasranekanayutā gaṅgā yathā vālikā
yaṣṭā bodhivaṭāsitena bhavatā tenādya vibhrājase || Verse 23.22

SC 30bhāryā ceṣṭatamā sutāśca dayitā dāsyaśca
dāsāstathā udyānā nagarāṇi rāṣṭranigamā rājyāni sāntaḥpurāḥ |
hastā pādaśirottamāṅgamapi vā cakṣūṁṣi jihvā tathā
tyaktā te varabodhicarya caratā tenādya vibhrājase || Verse 23.23

SC 31Vai 262uktaṁ yadvacanaṁ tvayā subahuśo buddho bhaviṣyāmyahaṁ
tāriṣye bahusattvakoṭinayutā duḥkhārṇavenohyatā |
dhyānādhīndriyabuddhibhiḥ kavacitaḥ saddharmanāvā svayaṁ
sā caiṣā pratipūrṇa tubhya praṇidhistāriṣyase prāṇinaḥ || Verse 23.24

SC 32yatpuṇyaṁ ca stavitva vādivṛṣabhaṁ lokasya cakṣurdadaṁ
sarve bhūtva udagrahṛṣṭamanasaḥ prārthema sarvajñatām |
samudānītva varāgrabodhimatulāṁ buddhaiḥ susaṁvarṇitāṁ
evaṁ tadvinihatya mārapariṣāṁ buddhema sarvajñatām || Verse 23.25

SC 33evaṁ khalu bhikṣavo māraputrāstathāgatamabhiṣṭutyaikānte tasthuḥ prāñjalayastathāgataṁ namasyantaḥ ||

SC 34atha khalu paranirmitavaśavartī devaputro ’nekairdevaputraśatasahasraiḥ parivṛtaḥ puraskṛto jāmbūnadasuvarṇavarṇaiḥ padmaistathāgatamabhyavakīrya saṁmukhamābhirgāthābhirabhyastāvīt—

SC 35apīḍita aluḍita avitathavacanā apagatatamaraja amṛtagatigatā |
arahasi divi bhuvi śriyakriyamatulā atidyutismṛtimati praṇipati śirasā || Verse 23.26

SC 36ratikara raṇajaha rajamalamathanā ramayasi suranara suviśadavacanaiḥ |
vikasita suvipula varatanu kiraṇaiḥ suranarapatiriva jayasi jagadidam || Verse 23.27

SC 37paragaṇipramathana paracarikuśalā priyu bhava naramaru paramati dhunatā |
paracari vibhajasi sunipuṇamatimān pathi iha vicaratu daśabalagamane || Verse 23.28

SC 38tyaji pṛthu bhavagrahi vitathadukha mahā vinayasi suranara yathamati vinaye |
vicarasi catudiśa śaśiriva gagane cakṣu bhava parāyaṇa iha bhuvi tribhave || Verse 23.29

SC 39priyu bhava naramaru na ca khali viṣaye ramayasi śubharati kāmarativirato |Vai 263
dinadarśi pariṣadi na ti samu tribhave nāthu gati parāyaṇu tvamiha hi jagataḥ || Verse 23.30

SC 40evaṁ khalu bhikṣavo vaśavartidevapramukhāḥ parinirmitavaśavartino devaputrāstathāgatamabhiṣṭutyaikānte tasthuḥ prāñjalayastathāgataṁ namasyantaḥ ||

SC 41atha khalu sunirmito devaputro devasaṁghaparivṛtaḥ puraskṛto nānāratnapaṭṭadāmaistathāgatamabhichādya saṁmukhamābhirgāthābhirabhyastāvīt—

SC 42dharmāloka bhavān samudgata trividhamalanucchido
mohādṛṣṭiavidyaghātako hiriśiribharitaḥ |
mithyāmārgaratāmimāṁ prajāmamṛte thapayito
utpanno iha loki cetiyo divi bhuvi mahitaḥ || Verse 23.31

SC 43tvaṁ vaidyo kuśalacikitsako hyamṛtasukhadado
dṛṣṭikleśamavidyasaṁcayaṁ purimamanuśayam |
sarvavyādhyapanesi dehināṁ purimajinapathe
tasmādvaidyatamo ’si nāyakā vicarasi dharaṇīm || Verse 23.32

SC 44candrāsūryaprabhāśca jyotiṣā maṇi tatha jvalanā
śakrabrahmaprabhā na bhāsate puratu śirighane |
prajñālokakarā prabhaṁkarā prabhasiribharitā
pratyakṣāstava jñāti adbhute praṇipati śirasā || Verse 23.33

SC 45satyāsatyakathī vināyakā sumadhuravacanā
dāntā śāntamanā jitendriya praśamitamanasā |
śāstā śāsaniyāṁ praśāsase naramarupariṣāṁ
vande śākyamuniṁ nararṣabhaṁ suranaramahitam || Verse 23.34

SC 46jñāniṁ jñānakathāgradhārakā jñapayasi tribhave
traividyatrivimokṣadeśakā trimalamalanudā |
bhavyābhavya mune prajānase yathamati vinaye
vande tvāṁ trisahasri adbhutaṁ divi bhuvi mahitam || Verse 23.35

SC 47evaṁ khalu bhikṣavaḥ sunirmito devaputraḥ saparivārastathāgatamabhiṣṭutyaikānte ’sthāt prāñjalīkṛtastathāgataṁ namaskurvan ||

SC 48Vai 264atha khalu saṁtuṣito devaputraḥ sārdhaṁ tuṣitakāyikairdevairyena tathāgatastenopasaṁkrāmat | upasaṁkramya mahatā divyavastrajālena bodhimaṇḍaniṣaṇṇaṁ tathāgatamabhisaṁchādya saṁmukhamābhirgāthābhirabhyastauṣīt—

SC 49tuṣitālayi yadvasitastvaṁ tatra ti deśitu dharma udāro |
na ca chidyati sā anuśāsti adyapi dharmacarī suraputrā || Verse 23.36

SC 50na ca darśana tṛpti labhāmo dharma śṛṇotu na vindati tṛptim |
guṇasāgara lokapradīpā vandima te śirasā manasā ca || Verse 23.37

SC 51tuṣitālaya yaccalitastvaṁ śoṣita akṣaṇa sarvi tadā te |
yada bodhivaṭe upaviṣṭaḥ sarvajagasya kileśa praśāntāḥ || Verse 23.38

SC 52yasya kṛtena ca bodhi udārā eṣa ti prāpti jinitvana māram |
tvā praṇidhī tapasā paripūrṇā kṣipra pravartaya cakramudāram || Verse 23.39

SC 53bahu dikṣiṣu prāṇisahasrā dharmaratā kṣuṇiyāmatha dharmam |
kṣipra pravartaya cakramudāraṁ mocaya prāṇisahasra bhaveṣu || Verse 23.40

SC 54evaṁ khalu bhikṣavaḥ saṁtuṣito devaputraḥ saparivārastathāgatamabhiṣṭatyaikānte ’sthāt prāñjalīkṛtastathāgataṁ namasyamānaḥ ||

SC 55atha khalu suyāmadevaputrapramukhāḥ suyāmā devā yena tathāgatastenopasaṁkrāmat |
upasaṁkramya nānāpuṣpadhūpagandhamālyavilepanairbodhimaṇḍaniṣaṇṇaṁ tathāgataṁ saṁpūjya saṁmukhamābhiḥ sārūpyābhirgāthābhistuṣṭavuḥ—

SC 56sadṛśo ’sti na te kutontare śīla samādhi tathaiva prajñayā |
adhimuktivimuktikovidā śirasā vandima te tathāgatam || Verse 23.41

SC 57Vai 265dṛṣṭā sa viyūha śobhanā bodhimaṇḍasmi marubhi yā kṛtā |
na tamarhati anya kaścanā yatha tvaṁ devamanuṣyapūjitaḥ || Verse 23.42

SC 58na mudhāya bhavān samudgato yasya arthe bahu cīrṇa duṣkarā |
vijito hi śaṭhaḥ sasainyakaḥ prāptā bodhi anuttarā tvayā || Verse 23.43

SC 59āloka kṛto daśādiśe prajñādīpena triloka jvālitaḥ |
timiraṁ apanāyayiṣyase dāsyasi cakṣuranuttaraṁ jage || Verse 23.44

SC 60bahukalpa stuvanti bhāṣato romarūpasya na cāntu asti te |
guṇasāgara lokaviśrutā śirasā vandima te tathāgatam || Verse 23.45

SC 61evaṁ khalu te suyāmadevaputrapramukhā devāstathāgatamabhiṣṭutyaikānte tasthuḥ prāñjalastathāgataṁ namasyantaḥ ||

SC 62atha khalu śakro devānāmindraḥ sārdhaṁ trāyatriṁśakāyikairdevairnānāpuṣpadhūpadīpagandhamālyavilepanacūrṇacīvarachatradhvajapatākāvyūhaistathāgataṁ saṁpūjya ābhirgāthābhirabhyastāvīt—

SC 63askhalitā anavadyā sadā susthitā merukalpā mune
daśadiśi suvighuṣṭa jñānaprabhā puṇyatejānvitā |
buddhaśatasahasra saṁpūjitā pūrvi tubhyaṁ mune
tasya viśeṣu yena bodhidrume mārasenā jitā || Verse 23.46

SC 64śīlaśrutasamādhiprajñākarā jñānaketudhvajā
jaramaraṇanighāti vaidyottamā lokacakṣurdadā |
trimalakhilaprahīṇa śāntendriyā śāntacittā mune
śaraṇu tavamupema śākyarṣabhā dharmarājā jage || Verse 23.47

SC 65bodhicarī anantatulyā abhūdvīryasthāmodgatā
prajñābala upāya maitrābalaṁ brāhmapuṇyaṁ balam |Vai 266
eti balamanantatulyā bhavaṁ bodhi saṁprasthite
daśabalabaladhārī adyā punarbodhimaṇḍe bhuto || Verse 23.48

SC 66dṛṣṭva camu anantasattve surā bhītatrastābhavan
mā khu śramaṇarāju bādhiṣyate bodhimaṇḍe sthitaḥ |
na ca bhavatu babhūva tebhyo bhayaṁ no ca kāyeñjanā
karahata gurubhāra saṁkampanā mārasenā jitā || Verse 23.49

SC 67yatha ca purimakebhi siṁhāsane prāpta bodhi varā
tatha tvayā anubuddha tulyā samā anyathā tvaṁ na hi |
samamanasa samacitta sarvajñatā sthāma prāptaṁ tvayā
tena bhava svayaṁbhu lokottamo puṇyakṣetraṁ jage || Verse 23.50

SC 68evaṁ khalu bhikṣavaḥ śakro devānāmindraḥ sārdhaṁ devaputraistrāyatriṁśaistathāgatamabhiṣṭutyaikānte ’sthāt prāñjalīkṛtastathāgataṁ namaskurvan ||

SC 69atha khalu catvāro mahārājānaḥ sārdhaṁ caturmahārājakāyikairdevaputrairyena tathāgatastenopasaṁkrāmat | upasaṁkramyābhimuktakacampakasumanāvārṣikadhānuskārimālyadāmaparigṛhītā apsaraḥśatasahasraparivṛtā divyasaṁgītisaṁpravāditena tathāgatasya pūjāṁ kṛtvā ābhiḥ sārūpyābhirgāthābhistuṣṭuvuḥ—

SC 70sumadhuravacanā manojñaghoṣā śaśi va praśāntikarā prasannacittā |
prahasitavadanā prabhūtajihvā paramasuprītikarā mune namaste || Verse 23.51

SC 71rutaravita ya asti sarvaloke sumadhura premaṇiyā narāmarūṇām |
bhavata svaru pramukta mañjughoṣo abhibhavate ruta sarvi bhāṣamāṇāṁ || Verse 23.52

SC 72rāgu samayi doṣamohakleśā prīti janeti amānuṣāṁ viśuddhām |
akaluṣahṛdayā niśāmya dharmaṁ ārya vimukti labhanti te hi sarve || Verse 23.53

SC 73na ca bhava atimanyase avidvāṁ na ca puna vidvamadena jātu mattaḥ |Vai 267
unnatu na ca naiva conatastvaṁ giririva susthitu sāgarasya madhye || Verse 23.54

SC 74lābha iha sulabdha mānuṣāṇāṁ yatra hi tādṛśu jātu sattva loke |
śrīriva padumo dhanasya dātrī tatha tava dāsyati dharmu sarvaloke || Verse 23.55

SC 75evaṁ khalu caturmahārājapramukhā mahārājakāyikā devā bodhimaṇḍaniṣaṇṇaṁ tathāgatamabhiṣṭutyaikānte tasthuḥ prāñjalayastathāgataṁ namasyantaḥ ||

SC 76atha khalvantarikṣā devāstathāgatasyāntikamupasaṁkramyābhisaṁbodheḥ pūjākarmaṇe sarvamantarīkṣaṁ ratnajālena kiṅkiṇījālena ratnachatrai ratnapatākābhī ratnapaṭṭadāmai ratnāvataṁsakairvividhamuktāhārapuṣpadāmārdhakāyikadevatāparigṛhītairardhacandrakaiśca samalaṁkṛtya tathāgatāya niryātayanti sma | niryātya ca saṁmukhamābhirgāthābhirabhyastāviṣuḥ—

SC 77asmāku vāsaṁ gagane dhruvaṁ mune paśyāma sattvā cariyā yathā jage |
bhavataścariṁ prekṣiya śuddhasattva skhalitaṁ na paśyāma tavaikacitte || Verse 23.56

SC 78ye āgatā pūjana bodhisattvā gaganaṁ sphuṭaṁ tairnaranāyakebhiḥ |
hānirvimānāna na cābhavanta tathā hi te vai gaganātmabhāvāḥ || Verse 23.57

SC 79ye antarīkṣātu pravarṣi puṣpāṁ syāccūḍabandhā hi mahāsahasrā |
te tubhya kāye patitā aśeṣā nadyo yathā sāgari saṁpraviṣṭāḥ || Verse 23.58

SC 80paśyāma chatrāṇyavataṁsakā ca mālāguṇāṁ campakapuṣpadāmāṁ |
hārāṁśca candrāṁśca tathārdhacandrāṁ kṣipanti devā na ca saṁskaroti || Verse 23.59

SC 81vālasya nābhūdavakāśamasmin devaiḥ sphuṭaṁ sarvata antarīkṣam |
kurvanti pūjāṁ dvipadottamasya na ca te mado jāyati vismayo vā || Verse 23.60

SC 82Vai 268evaṁ khalvantarīkṣadevā bodhimaṇḍe niṣaṇṇaṁ tathāgatamabhiṣṭutyaikānte ’vatasthivantaḥ prāñjalayastathāgataṁ namasyantaḥ ||

SC 83atha khalu bhaumā devāstathāgatasya pūjākarmaṇe sarvāvantaṁ dharaṇītalaṁ suśodhitopaliptaṁ gandhodakapariṣiktaṁ puṣpāvakīrṇaṁ ca kṛtvā nānādūṣyavitānavitataṁ ca tathāgatāya niryātayanti sma | ābhirgāthābhirabhituṣṭuvuḥ—

SC 84vajramiva abhedyā saṁsthitā triḥsahasrā vajramayapadenāyaṁ sthito bodhimaṇḍe |
iha mama tvacamāṁsaṁ śuṣyatāmasthimajjā na ca ahu aspṛśitvā bodhi uttheṣya asmāt || Verse 23.61

SC 85savibhava narasiṁhā sarviyaṁ triḥsahasrā na kariṣu adhisthānaṁ syādvidīrṇaśeṣā |
tādṛśa mahavegā āgatā bodhisattvā yeṣa kramatalebhiḥ kampitā kṣetrakoṭyaḥ || Verse 23.62

SC 86lābha iha sulabdhā bhūmidevairudārā yatra paramasattvaścaṁkramī medinīye |
yatra ku raju loke sarva obhāsitāste cetibhu trisahasraḥ kiṁ punastubhya kāyaḥ || Verse 23.63

SC 87hesti śatasahasraṁ yāvataścāpaskandho dharaṇitalu jagasyā yāvataścopajīvyaḥ |
sarva vayu dharemo medinī triḥsahasrāṁ sarva tava dadāmo bhuṅkṣvimāṁ tvaṁ yatheṣṭam || Verse 23.64

SC 88yatra bhava sthihedvā caṁkramedvā śayedvā ye ’pi sugataputrāḥ śrāvakā gautamasya |
dharmakatha kathentī ye ’pi vā tāṁ śṛṇonti sarvakuśalamūlaṁ bodhiye nāmayāmaḥ || Verse 23.65
