• Lalitavistaraḥ |

16: bimbisāropasaṁkramaṇaparivartaḥ ṣoḍaśaḥ

SC 1Vai 174evaṁ khalu bhikṣavaśchandako bodhisattvādhisthānena rājñaḥ śuddhodanasya gopāyāḥ śākyakanyāyāśca sarvasya cāntaḥpurasya sarvasya ca śākyagaṇasya śokavinodakathāmakārṣīt ||

SC 2iti hi bhikṣavo bodhisattvo lubdhakarūpāya devaputrāya kāśikāni vastrāṇi dattvā tasya sakāśātkāṣāyāni vastrāṇi gṛhītvā svayameva pravajyāṁ lokānuvartanāmupādāya sattvānukampāyai sattvaparipācanārtham ||

SC 3atha bodhisattvo yenaiva śākyā brāhmaṇyā āśramastenopasaṁkrāmat | sā bodhisattvaṁ vāsena bhaktena copanimantrayate sma | tato bodhisattvaḥ padmāyā brāhmaṇyā āśramaṁ gacchati sma | tayāpi bodhisattvo vāsena bhaktena copanimantrito ’bhūt ||

SC 4tato raivatasya brahmarṣerāśramamagamat | asāvapi bodhisattvaṁ tathaivopanimantrayate sma | tathaiva rājako ’pi datṛmadaṇḍikaputro bodhisattvamupanimantrayate sma ||

SC 5iti hi bhikṣavo bodhisattvo ’nupūrveṇa vaiśālīṁ mahānagarīmanuprāpto ’bhut ||

SC 6tena khalu punaḥ samayenārāḍaḥ kālāpo vaiśālīmupanisṛtya prativasati sma mahatā śrāvakasaṁghena sārdhaṁ tribhiḥ śiṣyaśataiḥ | sa śiṣyebhya ākiṁcanyāyatanasahavratāyai dharmaṁ deśayati sma | sa bodhisattvaṁ dūrata evāgacchantaṁ dṛṣṭvā āścaryaprāptaḥ śiṣyānāmantrayate sma—paśyata paśyata bho rūpamasyeti | te ’bruvan—evaṁ hyetatpaśyāmaḥ | enamativismayanīyam ||

SC 7tato ’haṁ bhikṣavo yenārāḍaḥ kālāpastenopasaṁkramyārāḍa kālāpametadavocat—careyamahaṁ bho ārāḍe kālāpe brahmacaryam | so ’vocat—cara bho gautama tathārūpeṇa dharmākhyāne yasmin śrāddhaḥ kulaputro ’lpakṛcchreṇājñāmārādhayati ||

SC 8tasya me bhikṣava etadabhūt—asti me chando ’sti vīryamasti smṛtirasti samādhirasti prajñā, yannvahameko ’pramatta ātāpī vyapakṛṣṭo vihareyaṁ tasyaiva dharmasya prāptaye sākṣātkriyāyai ||

SC 9atha khalvahaṁ bhikṣavo eko ’pramatta ātāpī vyapakṛṣṭo viharannalpakṛcchreṇaivaṁ taṁ dharmamadhyavagacchan sākṣādakārṣam ||

SC 10atha khalvahaṁ bhikṣavo yenārāḍaḥ kālāpastenopasaṁkramyaitadavocat—etāvadbho tvayā ārāḍa dharmo ’dhigataḥ sākṣātkṛtaḥ? so ’vocat—evametadbho gautama | tamahamavocat—mayāpi bho eṣa dharmaḥ sākṣātkṛto ’dhigataḥ | so ’vocat—tena hi bho gautama yadahaṁ dharma jānāmi, bhavānapi taṁ jānāti, yaṁ bhavān jānāti, ahamapi taṁ jānāmi | tena hyāvāmubhāvapīmaṁ śiṣyagaṇaṁ pariharāvaḥ ||

SC 11iti hi bhikṣava ārāḍaḥ kālāpaḥ paramayā pūjayā māṁ pūjayati sma | antevāsiṣu ca māṁ samānārthatayā sthāpayati sma ||Vai 175

SC 12tasya me bhikṣava etadabhūt—ayaṁ khalvārāḍasya dharmo na nairyāṇiko na niryāti, tatkatarasya samyagduḥkhakṣayāya? yannvahamata uttari paryeṣamāṇaścareyam ||

SC 13atha khalvahaṁ bhikṣavo yathābhirāmaṁ vaiśālyāṁ vihṛtya magadheṣu ca prakrānto ’bhūt | so ’haṁ magadheṣu caryāṁ caran yena māgadhakānāṁ rājagṛhaṁ nagaraṁ tadanusṛto yena ca pāṇḍavaḥ parvatarājastenopasaṁkrānto ’bhūvam | tatrāhaṁ pāṇḍave parvatarājapārśve vyāhārṣamekākyadvitīyo ’sahāyo ’nekairdevakoṭinayutaśatasahasraiḥ saṁrakṣitaḥ ||

SC 14tato ’haṁ kalyameva saṁnivāsya pātracīvaramādāya tapodadvāreṇa rājagṛhaṁ mahānagaraṁ piṇḍāya prāvikṣat prāsādikenābhikrāntena pratikrāntena vyavalokitena saṁmiñjitena prasāritena prāsādikena saṁghāṭīpaṭapātracīvaradhāraṇenāvikṣiptairindriyairabahirgatena mānasena nirmitavattailapātradharavadyugamātraṁ paśyan | tatra māṁ rājagṛhakā manuṣyā dṛṣṭvā vismitā abhūvan—kiṁ svidayaṁ brahmā bhaviṣyati śakro devānāmindra āhosvidvaiśravaṇo āhosvitkiṁcidgiridaivatam ||

SC 15tatredamucyate—

SC 16atha vimaladharo hyanantatejo svayamiha pravrajiyāna bodhisattvaḥ |
śāntamanu dānta īryavanto viharati pāṇḍavaśailarājapārśve || Verse 16.1

SC 17rajani vigatu jñātva bodhisattvaḥ paramasudarśaniyaṁ nivāsayitvā |
pātra pratigṛhīya nīcamāno praviśati rājagṛhaṁ sapiṇḍapātram || Verse 16.2

SC 18kanakamiva sudhātujātarūpaṁ kavacitu lakṣaṇatriṁśatā dvibhiśca |
naragaṇa tatha nāri prekṣamāṇo na ca bhavate kvaci tṛpti darśanena || Verse 16.3

SC 19vīthi racita ratnavastradhāryai avaśiriyā janu yāti pṛṣṭhato ’sya |
ko nu ayu adṛṣṭapūrvasattvo yasya prabhāya puraṁ vibhāti sarvam || Verse 16.4

SC 20upari sthihiya nāriṇāṁ sahasrā tathariva dvāri tathaiva vātayāne |Vai 176
rathya bharita gehi śūnya kṛtvā naravaru prekṣiṣu te ananyakarmāḥ || Verse 16.5

SC 21na ca bhuyu krayavikrayaṁ karontī na ca puna sauṇḍa pibanti madyapānam |
na ca gṛhi na ca vīthiye ramante puruṣavarasya nirīkṣamāṇa rūpam || Verse 16.6

SC 22puruṣa tvaritu gacchi rājagehaṁ avaciṣu rāja sa bimbisāra tuṣṭo |
deva parama tubhya labdha lābhā svayamiha brahma pure carāti piṇḍam || Verse 16.7

SC 23keci avaci śakra devarājo apari bhaṇanti suyāma devaputraḥ |
tatha api saṁtuṣitaṁ va nirmitaśca apari bhaṇanti sunirmiteṣu devaḥ || Verse 16.8

SC 24keci puna bhaṇanti candrasūryau tathapi ca rāhu baliśca vemacitrī |
keci puna bhaṇanti vācamevaṁ ayu so pāṇḍavaśailarājavāsī || Verse 16.9

SC 25vacanamimu śruṇitva pārthivo ’sau paramaudagramanā sthito gavākṣe |
prekṣati varasattva bodhisattvaṁ jvalatu śirīya sudhātukāñcanaṁ vā || Verse 16.10

SC 26piṇḍa dadiya rāja bimbisāraḥ puruṣamavocannirīkṣa kva prayātī |
dṛṣṭva girivaraṁ sa gacchamāno avaciṣu deva gataḥ sa śailapārśvam || Verse 16.11

SC 27rajani vigatu jñātva bimbisāro mahata janaiḥ parivārito narendraḥ |
upagami pāṇḍavaśailarājamūle śiriya jvalantu tamadṛśāti śailam || Verse 16.12

SC 28Vai 177dharaṇi vrajitu yāni oruhitvā paramasugaurava prekṣi bodhisattvam |
meruriva yathā hyakampamāno nyasiya tṛṇāni niṣaṇṇa sostikena || Verse 16.13

SC 29śirasi caraṇi vandayitva rājā vividhakathāṁ samudāharitva vocat |
dadami tava upārdhu sarvarājyād rama iha kāmaguṇairahaṁ ca piṇḍam || Verse 16.14

SC 30prabhaṇati giri bodhisattva ślakṣṇaṁ dharaṇipate ciramāyu pālayasva |
ahamapi pravijahya rājyamiṣṭaṁ pravrajito nirapekṣi śāntihetoḥ || Verse 16.15

SC 31daharu taruṇayauvanairupetaḥ śubhatanuvarṇanibho ’si vegaprāptaḥ |
vipula dhana pratīccha nārisaṁghaṁ iha mama rājyi vasāhi bhuṅkṣva kāmāṁ || Verse 16.16

SC 32paramapramudito ’smi daśanātte avaciṣu sa māgadharāja bodhisattvam |
bhavahi mama sahāyu sarvarājyaṁ ahu tava dāsyi prabhūta bhuṅkṣva kāmāṁ || Verse 16.17

SC 33mā ca puna vane vasāhi śūnye ma bhuyu tṛṇeṣu vasāhi bhūmivāsam |
paramasukumāru tubhya kāyo iha mama rājyi vasāhi bhuṅkṣva kāmāṁ || Verse 16.18

SC 34prabhaṇati giri bodhisattva ślakṣṇaṁ akuṭila premaṇiyā hitānukampī |
svasti dharaṇipāla te ’stu nityaṁ na ca ahu kāmaguṇebhirarthiko ’smi || Verse 16.19

SC 35kāma viṣasamā anantadoṣā narakaprapātana pretatiryagyonau |
vidubhi vigarhita cāpyanārya kāmā jahita mayā yathā pakvakheṭapiṇḍam || Verse 16.20

SC 36Vai 178kāma drumaphalā yathā patantī yathamiva abhrabalāhakā vrajanti |
adhruva capalagāmi mārutaṁ vā vikiraṇa sarvaśubhasya vañcanīyā || Verse 16.21

SC 37kāma alabhamāna dahyayante tatha api labdha na tṛpti vindayantī |
yada puna avaśasya bhakṣayante tada mahaduḥkha janenti ghora kāmāḥ || Verse 16.22

SC 38kāma dharaṇipāla ye ca divyā tatha api mānuṣa kāma ye praṇītā |
eku naru labheta sarvakāmāṁ na ca so tṛpti labheta bhūyu eṣan || Verse 16.23

SC 39ye tu dharaṇipāla śāntadāntā ārya anāśrava dharmapūrṇasaṁjñā |
prajñaviduṣa tṛpta te sutṛptā na ca puna kāmaguṇeṣu kāci tṛptiḥ || Verse 16.24

SC 40kāma dharaṇipāla sevamānā purima na vidyati koṭi saṁskṛtasya |
lavaṇajala yathā hi nārū pītvā bhuyu tṛṣa vardhati kāma sevamāne || Verse 16.25

SC 41api ca dharaṇipāla paśya kāyaṁ adhruvamasāraku duḥkhayantrametat |
navabhi vraṇamukhaiḥ sadā sravantaṁ na mama narādhipa kāmachandarāgaḥ || Verse 16.26

SC 42ahamapi vipulāṁ vijahya kāmāṁ tathapi ca istrisahasra darśanīyāṁ |
anabhiratu bhaveṣu nirgato ’haṁ paramaśivāṁ varabodhi prāptukāmaḥ || Verse 16.27

SC 43rājā āha—

SC 44katama diśi kuto gato ’si bhikṣo kva ca tava janma kva te pitā kva mātā |Vai 179
kṣatriya atha brāhmaṇo ’tha rājā parikatha bhikṣu yadī na bhārasaṁjñā || Verse 16.28

SC 45bodhisattva āha—

SC 46śratu ti dharaṇipāla śākiyānāṁ kapilapuraṁ paramaṁ suṛddhisphītam |
pitu mama śuddhodaneti nāmnā tanu ahu pravrajito guṇābhilāṣī || Verse 16.29

SC 47rājā āha—

SC 48sādhu tava sudṛṣṭadarśanaṁ te yanu tava janma vayaṁ pi tasya śiṣyāḥ |
api ca mama kṣamasva āśayenā yamapi nimantritu kāmavītarāgo || Verse 16.30

SC 49yadi tvaya anuprāptu bhoti bodhiḥ tada mama seti bhoti dharmasvāmim |
api ca mama purā sulabdha lābhā mama vijite vasasīha yatsvayaṁbho || Verse 16.31

SC 50punarapi caraṇāni vandayitvā kṛtva pradakṣiṇu gauraveṇa rājā |
svakajanaparivārito narendraḥ punarapi rājagṛhaṁ anupraviṣṭaḥ || Verse 16.32

SC 51magadhapuri praveśi lokanātho vihariya śāntamanā yathābhiprāyam |
arthu kariya devamānuṣāṇāṁ upagami tīru nirañjanā narendraḥ || Verse 16.33

SC 52|| iti śrīlalitavistare bimbisāropasaṁkramaṇaparivarto nāma ṣoḍaśamo ’dhyāyaḥ ||
