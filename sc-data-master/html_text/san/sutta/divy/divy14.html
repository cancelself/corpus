14. sūkarikāvadānam

Vai 120.001 dharmatā khalu cyavanadharmaṇo devaputraysa pañca pūrvanimittāni prādurbhavanti—akliṣṭāni vāsāṁsi kliśyanti, amlānāni mālyāni mlāyanti, daurgandhaṁ kāyena niṣkrāmati, ubhābhyāṁ kakṣābhyāṁ svedaḥ prādurbhavanti, cyavanadharmā devaputraḥ sva āsane dhṛtiṁ na labhate |
Vai 120.003 athānyatamaścyavanadharmā devaputraḥ pṛthivyāmāvartate, saṁparivartyaivaṁ cāha—hā mandākinī, hā puṣkariṇī, hā vāpī, hā caitraratha, hā pāruṣyaka, hā nandanavana, hā miśrakāvana, hā pāriyātraka, hā puṇḍukambalaśilā, hā devasabhā, hā sudarśana, iti karuṇakaruṇaṁ paridevate sma |
Vai 120.007 adrākṣīcchakro devānāmindrastaṁ devaputramatyarthaṁ pṛthivyāmāvartantaṁ parivartantam |
Vai 120.008 dṛṣṭvā punaryena sa devaputrastenopasaṁkrāntaḥ |
Vai 120.009 upasaṁkramya taṁ devaputramidamavocat—hā kasmāt tvaṁ mārṣa atyarthaṁ pṛthivyāmāvartase, saṁparivartase, karuṇakaruṇaṁ paridevase—hā mandākinī, hā puṣkiriṇī, hā vāpī, hā caitraratha, hā pāruṣyaka, hā nandanavana, hā miśrakāvana, hā pāriyātraka, hā pāṇḍukambalaśilā, hā devasabhā, hā sudarśana iti karuṇakaruṇaṁ paridevase? evamukte devaputraḥ śakraṁ devānāmindramidamavocat—eṣo ’haṁ kauśika divyaṁ sukhamanubhūya itaḥ saptame divase rājagṛhe nagare sūkaryāḥ kukṣau upapatsyāmi |
Vai 120.014 tatra mayā bahūni varṣāṇyuccāraprasrāvaḥ paribhoktavya iti |
atha śakro devānāmindraḥ kāruṇyatayā taṁ devaputramidamavocat—ehi tvaṁ mārṣa, buddhaṁ śaraṇaṁ gaccha dvipadānāmagryam, dharmaṁ śaraṇaṁ gaccha virāgāṇāmagryam, saṁghaṁ śaraṇaṁ gaccha gaṇānāmagryamiti |
Vai 120.016 atha sa devaputrastiryagyonyupapattibhayabhīto maraṇabhayabhītaśca śakraṁ devānāmindramidamavocat—eṣo ’haṁ kauśika buddhaṁ śaraṇaṁ gacchāmi dvipadānāmagryam, dharmaṁ śaraṇaṁ gacchāmi virāgāṇāmagryam, saṁghaṁ śaraṇaṁ gacchāmi gaṇānāmagryam |
Vai 120.019 atha sa devaputrastriśaraṇaparigṛhīto bhūtvā cyutaḥ kālagatastuṣite devanikāye upapannaḥ |
|
Vai 120.021 dharmatā khalu adhastāddevānāṁ jñānadarśanaṁ pravartate nordhvam |
atha śakro devānāmindrastaṁ devaputramavalokayati—kimasau devaputraḥ sūkarikāyāḥ kukṣau upapanno na veti |
Vai 120.022 yāvat paśyati—nopapannastiryakpreteṣu |
Vai 120.023 narakeṣūpapanna iti paśyati |
nopapannaḥ |
manuṣyāṇāṁ sabhāgatāyāmupapanna iti paśyati |
Vai 120.024 nopapannaḥ |
cāturmahārājakāyikān devāṁstrāyastriṁśāṁścāvalokayitumārabdhaḥ |
Vai 120.025 tatrāpi nādrākṣīt |
atha śakro devānāmindraḥ kutūhalajāto yena bhagavāṁstenopasaṁkrāntaḥ |
Vai 120.026 upasaṁkramya bhagavataḥ pādau śirasā vanditvā ekānte niṣaṇṇaḥ |
ekāntaniṣaṇṇaḥ śakro devānāmindro bhagavantamidamavocat—ihāhaṁ bhadanta adrākṣamanyatamaṁ devaputraṁ cyavanadharmāṇaṁ pṛthivyāmāvartamānaṁ karuṇakaruṇaṁ ca paridevamānam—hā mandākini, hā puṣkiriṇi, hā vāpi, hā caitraratha, hā pāruṣyaka, hā nandanavana, hā miśrakāvana, hā pāriyātraka, hā pāṇḍukambalāśilā, hā devasabhā, hā sudarśana iti |
Vai 120.029 tamenamevaṁ vadāmi—kasmāt tvaṁ mārṣa atyarthaṁ śocasi paridevase krandasi urasi tāḍayasi saṁmohāpadyasa iti? sa evamāha—eṣo ’haṁ kauśika divyaṁ sukhamapahāya itaḥ saptame divase rājagṛhe nagare sūkarikāyāḥ kukṣau upapatsyāmi |
Vai 120.031 tatra mayā Vai 121.000bahūni varṣāṇi uccāraprasrāvaḥ paribhoktavyaṁ bhaviṣyati | Vai 121.001

tamenamevaṁ vadāmi—ehi tvaṁ mārṣa buddhaṁ śaraṇaṁ gaccha dvipadānāmagryam, dharmaṁ śaraṇaṁ gaccha virāgāṇāmagryam, saṁghaṁ śaraṇaṁ gaccha gaṇānāmagryamiti |
Vai 121.003 sa evamāha—eṣo ’haṁ kauśika buddhaṁ śaraṇaṁ gacchāmi dvipadānāmagryam, dharmaṁ śaraṇaṁ gacchāmi virāgāṇāmagryam, saṁghaṁ śaraṇaṁ gacchāmi gaṇānāmagryam |
Vai 121.004 ityuktvā sa devaputraḥ kālagataḥ |
Vai 121.005 kutrāsau bhadanta devaputra upapannah? bhagavānāha—tuṣitā nāma kauśika devāḥ sarvakāmasamṛddhayaḥ |
Vai 121.006 tatrāsau modate devo gatveha śaraṇatrayam |
atha śakro devānāmindra āttamānāstasyāṁ velāyāmimāṁ gāthāṁ gāthāṁ bhāṣate—Vai 121.008 ye buddhaṁ śaraṇam yānti na te gacchanti durgatim |
Vai 121.009 prahāya mānuṣān kāyān divyān kāyānupāsate (1)|
Vai 121.010 ye dharmaṁ śaraṇam yānti na te gacchanti durgatim |
Vai 121.011 prahāya mānuṣān kāyān divyān kāyānupāsate (2)|
Vai 121.012 ye saṁghaṁ śaraṇam yānti na te gacchanti durgatim |
Vai 121.013 prahāya mānuṣān kāyān divyān kāyānupāsate (3)|
Vai 121.014 atha bhagavāñ śakrasya devānāmindrasya bhāṣitamanusaṁvarṇayannevamāha—evametat kauśika, evametat |
Vai 121.016 ye buddhaṁ śaraṇam yānti na te gacchanti durgatim |
Vai 121.017 prahāya mānuṣān kāyān divyān kāyānupāsate (4)|
Vai 121.018 ye dharmaṁ śaraṇam yānti na te gacchanti durgatim |
Vai 121.019 prahāya mānuṣān kāyān divyān kāyānupāsate (5)|
Vai 121.020 ye saṁghaṁ śaraṇam yānti na te gacchanti durgatim |
Vai 121.021 prahāya mānuṣān kāyān divyān kāyānupāsate (6)|
Vai 121.022 atha śakro devānāmindro bhagavato bhāṣitamabhinandyānumodya bhagavataḥ pādau śirasā vanditvā bhagavantaṁ triḥ pradakṣiṇīkṛtya prāñjalikṛtasampuṭo bhagavantaṁ namasyamānastatraivāntarhitaḥ |
|
Vai 121.024 idamavocadbhagavān |
āttamanasaste bhikṣavo ’bhyanandan |
|
Vai 121.025 iti śrīdivyāvadāne sūkarikāvadānaṁ caturdaśamam |
