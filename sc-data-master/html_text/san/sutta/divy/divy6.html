6. indranāmabrāhmaṇāvadānam

Vai 047.000 bhagavāñ śrughnāmanuprāptaḥ |
Vai 047.001 śrughnāyāmindro nāma brāhmaṇaḥ prativasati |
Vai 047.002 sa ca rūpayauvanaśrutamanuprāpto na mamāsti kaścit tulya ityatīva vikatthate |
Vai 047.003 bhagavāṁścānyatamasmin pradeśe purastādbhikṣusaṁghasya prajñapta evāsane niṣaṇṇo dharmaṁ deśayati |
Vai 047.004 aśrauṣīdindro nāma brāhmaṇah—śramaṇo gautamaḥ śrughnāmanuprāpta iti |
Vai 047.005 tasyaitadabhavat—śramaṇo gautamaḥ śrūyate ’bhirūpo darśanīyaḥ prāsādika iti |
Vai 047.006 gacchāmi paśyāmi kiṁ mamāntikādabhirūpatara āhosvinneti |
Vai 047.007 sa nirgato yāvat paśyati bhagavantaṁ dvātriṁśatā mahāpuruṣalakṣaṇaiḥ samalaṁkṛtamaśītyā cānuvyañjanairvirājitagātraṁ vyāmaprabhālaṁkṛtaṁ sūryasahasarātirekaprabhaṁ jaṅgamamiva ratnaparvataṁ samantato bhadrakam |
Vai 047.009 dṛṣṭvā ca punarasyaitadabhavat—kiṁ cāpi śramaṇo gautamo mamāntikādabhirūpataraḥ, noccatara iti |
Vai 047.010 sa bhagavato mūrdhānamavalokayitumārabdho yāvanna paśyati |
sa ūrdhvataraṁ pradeśamārūḍhaḥ |
Vai 047.011 tatra bhagavānindraṁ brāhmaṇamāmantrayate—alaṁ brāhmaṇa, khedamāpatsyase |
yadi sumerumūrdhānamapi abhiruhya tathāgatasya mūrdhānamavalokayasi, tathā sutarāṁ khedamāpatsyase, na ca drakṣyasi |
Vai 047.013 api tu na tvayā śrutaṁ sasurāsurajagadanavalokitamūrdhāno buddhā bhagavanta iti? api tu yadīpsasi tathāgatasya śarīrapramāṇaṁ draṣṭum, tava gṛhe ’gnihotrakuṇḍaṁ tasyādhastādgośīrṣacandanamayī yaṣṭirupatiṣṭhate, tāmuddhṛtya māpaya |
Vai 047.015 tattathāgatamātāpaitṛkasyāśrayasya pramāṇamiti |
indro brāhmaṇaḥ saṁlakṣayati—etadasyāścaryaṁ na kadācinmayā śrutam, gacchāmi paśyāmīti |
Vai 047.016 tvaritatvaritagato ’gnihotrakuṇḍakasyādhastāt khanitumārabdhaḥ |
Vai 047.017 sarvaṁ tathaiva |
so ’bhiprasannaḥ |
sa saṁlakṣayati—nūnaṁ śramaṇo gautamaḥ sarvajñaḥ |
Vai 047.018 gacchāmi paryupāsitumiti |
sa prasādajāto yena bhagavāṁstenopasaṁkrāntaḥ |
Vai 047.019 upasaṁkramya bhagavatā sārdhaṁ saṁmukhaṁ saṁmodanīṁ saṁrañjanīṁ vividhāṁ kathāṁ vyatisārya ekānte niṣaṇṇaḥ |
Vai 047.020 tato bhagavatā āśayānuśayaṁ dhātuṁ prakṛtiṁ ca jñātvā tādṛśī caturāryasatyasamprativedhikī dharmadeśanā kṛtā, yathaindreṇa brāhmaṇena viṁśatiśikharasamudgataṁ satkāyadṛṣṭiśailaṁ jñānavajreṇa bhittvā srotāapattiphalaṁ sākṣātkṛtam |
Vai 047.022 sa dṛṣṭasatyaḥ kathayati—atikrānto ’haṁ bhadanta, atikrāntaḥ |
Vai 047.023 eṣo ’haṁ bhagavantaṁ śaraṇaṁ gacchāmi dharmaṁ ca bhikṣusaṁghaṁ ca |
upāsakaṁ ca māṁ dhāraya adyāgreṇa yāvajjīvaṁ prāṇopetaṁ śaraṇaṁ gatam |
Vai 047.024 abhiprasanno ’thendro brāhmaṇa utthāyāsanāt ekāṁsamuttarāsaṅgaṁ kṛtvā yena bhagavāṁstenāñjaliṁ praṇamya bhagavantamidamavocat—yadi bhagavānanujānīyāt, ahaṁ gośīrṣacandanamayyā yaṣṭyā mahaṁ prajñapayeyamiti |
Vai 047.026 bhagavānāha—gaccha brāhmaṇa anujñātaṁ prajñapayasi |
Vai 047.027 tatastena viviktāvakāśe mahatā satkāreṇāsau yaṣṭirucchrāpitā, mahaśca prajñapitaḥ |
Vai 047.028 anyairapi brāhmaṇagṛhapatibhiḥ kuśalamadhiṣṭhānāya bhavatviti viditvā kulā baddhā(?) |

Vai 048.005 evaṁ na karmaparihāṇir na puṇyaparihāṇiriti |
Vai 048.006 tena yathāgṛhītayaiva pratodayaṣṭyā tatrasthenaivābhivādanaṁ kṛtam—abhivādaye buddhaṁ bhagavantamiti |
Vai 048.007 tatra bhagavānāyuṣmantamānandamāmantrayate—bhavakṣayakaraḥ kṣaṇam |
eṣa brāhmaṇaḥ |
sacedasyaivaṁ samyaksampratyayajñānadarśanam ahamanenopakrameṇa vandito bhaveyam, evamanena dvābhyāṁ samyaksambuddhābhyāṁ vandanā kṛtā bhavet |
Vai 048.010 tatkasya hetoh? asminnānanda pradeśe kāśyapasya samyaksambuddhasyāvikopito ’sthisaṁghātastiṣṭhati |
Vai 048.011 athāyuṣmānānando laghuladhveva caturguṇamuttarāsaṅgaṁ prajñapya bhagavantamidamavocat—niṣīdatu bhagavān prajñapta evāsane |
Vai 048.012 evamayaṁ pṛthivīpradeśo dvābhyāṁ samyaksambuddhābhyāṁ paribhukto bhaviṣyati, yacca kāśyapena samyaksambuddhena, yaccaitarhi bhagavatā iti |
Vai 048.014 niṣaṇṇo bhagavān prajñapta evāsane |
niṣadya bhikṣūnāmantrayate sma—icchatha kālaḥ, etasya sugata samayaḥ, yaṁ bhagavān bhikṣūṇāṁ kāśyapasya samyaksambuddhasyāvikopitaṁ śarīrasaṁghātamupadarśayet |
Vai 048.017 dṛṣṭvā bhikṣavaścittamabhiprasādayiṣyanti |
tato bhagavatā laukikaṁ cittamutpāditam |
dharmatā khalu yasmin samaye buddhā bhagavanto laukikaṁ cittamutpādayanti, tasmin samaye kuntapipīlikā api prāṇino bhagavataścetasā cittamājānanti |
Vai 048.019 nāgāḥ saṁlakṣayanti—kiṁ kāraṇaṁ bhagavatā laukikacittamutpāditamiti? paśyanti—kāśyapasya samyaksambuddhasya śarīrasaṁghātamavikopitaṁ draṣṭukāma iti |
Vai 048.021 tatastaiḥ kāśyapasya samyaksambuddhasyāvikopitaśarīrasaṁghāta ucchrāpitaḥ |
tatra bhagavān bhikṣūnāmantrayate sma—udgṛhṇīta bhikṣavo nimittam |

Vai 048.023 rājñā prasenajitā śrutaṁ bhagavatā śrāvakāṇāṁ darśanāyāvikopitaṁ kāśyapasya samyaksambuddhasya śarīrasaṁghātaṁ samucchritamiti |
Vai 048.024 śrutvā ca punaḥ kutūhalajātaḥ sahāntaḥpureṇa kumārairamātyairbhaṭabalāgrair naigamajānapadaiśca draṣṭuṁ saṁprasthitaḥ |
Vai 048.025 evaṁ virūḍhakaḥ, anāthapiṇḍado gṛhapatiḥ, ṛṣidattaḥ purāṇasthapitaḥ, viśākhā mṛgāramātā, anekāni ca prāṇiśatasahasrāṇi kutūhalajātāni draṣṭuṁ saṁprasthitāni pūrvakaiśca kuśalamūlaiḥ saṁcodyamānāni |
Vai 048.027 yāvadasau anatarhitaḥ |
taiḥ śrutam—anatarhito ’sau bhagavataḥ kāśyapasya samyaksambuddhasya śarīrasaṁghāturavikopita iti |
Vai 048.029 śrutvā ca punasteṣāṁ duḥkhadaurmanasyamutpannam—vṛthā asmākamāgamanaṁ jātamiti |
athānyatamena copāsakena sa pradeśaḥ pradakṣiṇīkṛtaḥ |
