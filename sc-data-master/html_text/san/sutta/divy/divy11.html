11. aśokavarṇāvadānam

Vai 085.001 evaṁ mayā śrutam |
Vai 085.002 ekasmin samaye bhagavān satkṛto gurukṛto mānitaḥ pūjito rājabhī rājamātrairdhanibhiḥ pauraiḥ śreṣṭhibhiḥ sārthavāhairdevair nāgairasurairyakṣairgauḍaiḥ kinnarairmahoragairiti devanāgayakṣāsuragaruḍakinnaramahoragābhyarcito buddho bhagavāñ jñāto mahāpuṇyo lābhī cīvarapiṇḍapātaśayanāsanaglānapratyayabhaiṣajyapariṣkārāṇāṁ saśrāvakasaṁgho vaiśālyāṁ viharati sma markaṭasahasradatīre kūṭāgāraśālāyām |
Vai 085.006 tena khalu samayena vaiśālikā licchavaya idamevamrūpaṁ kriyākāramakārṣuh—pañcadaśyāṁ bhavantaḥ pakṣasya aṣṭamyāaṁ caturdaśyāṁ ca prāṇino hantavyā yatkāraṇameyurmanuṣyā māṁsamanveṣanta iti |
Vai 085.008 tena khalu samayena anyatamo goghātako mahāntaṁ vṛṣabhamādāya nagarānniṣkramati praghātayitum |
Vai 085.009 tamenaṁ mahājanakāyaḥ pṛṣṭhataḥ pṛṣṭhataḥ samanubaddho māṁsārthī kathayati—śīghramenaṁ vṛṣaṁ ghātaya, vayaṁ māṁsenārthina iti |
Vai 085.010 sa kathayati—evaṁ kariṣyāmi, kiṁ tu muhūrtamudīkṣadhvamiti |
Vai 085.011 tato vṛṣa īdṛśamanāryaṁ vaco duruktaṁ śrutvā bhītastrastaḥ saṁvignah āhṛṣṭaromakūpa itaścāmutaśca saṁbhrānto nirīkṣate, cintayati ca—ko māṁ kṛcchrasaṁkaṭasambādhaprāptamatrāṇamaśaraṇamiṣṭena jīvitenācchādayediti |
Vai 085.013 sa caivaṁ vihvalavadanastrāṇānveṣī tiṣṭhati |
Vai 085.014 bhagavāṁśca pūrvāhṇe nivāsya pātracīvaramādāya bhikṣugaṇaparivṛto bhikṣusaṁghapuraskṛto vaiśālīṁ piṇḍāya prāviśat |
Vai 085.015 athāsau dadarśa buddhaṁ bhagavantaṁ dvātriṁśatā mahāpuruṣalakṣaṇaiḥ samalaṁkṛtamaśītyānuvyañjanairvirājitagātraṁ vyāmaprabhālaṁkṛtaṁ sūryasahasrātirekaprabhaṁ jaṅgamamiva ratnaparvataṁ samantato bhadrakamam |
Vai 085.017 sahadarśanaiścāsya bhagavato ’ntike cittamabhiprasannam |
prasannacittaśca saṁlakṣayati—prāsādiko ’yaṁ sattvaviśeṣaḥ |
Vai 085.018 śakṣyatyeṣo mama prāṇaparitrāṇaṁ kartum |
Vai 085.019 yannvahamenamupasaṁkrameyamiti |
atha sa vṛṣo bhagavatyavekṣāvān pratibaddhacittah eṣo me śaraṇamiti sahasaiva tāni dṛḍhāni varatrakāṇi bandhanāni cchittvā pradhāvan yena bhagavāṁstenopasaṁkrāntaḥ |
Vai 085.021 upasaṁkramyobhābhyāṁ jānubhyāṁ bhagavataḥ pādayor nipatya pādau jihvayā nileḍhumārabdhaḥ |
Vai 085.022 sa cāsya raudrakarmā goghātakaḥ pṛṣṭhataḥ pṛṣṭhataḥ samanubaddha eva śastravyagrahastaḥ |
tato bhagavāṁstaṁ raudrakarmāṇaṁ goghātakamidamavocat—kuruṣva tvaṁ bhoḥ puruṣa anena govṛṣabheṇa sārdhaṁ sātmyam |
Vai 085.024 jīvitenācchādayeti |
sa kathayati—nāhaṁ bhadanta prabhavābhyenaṁ jīvitenācchādayitum |
tatkasya hetoh? mayā eṣa bahunā mūlyena krītaḥ |
Vai 085.025 putradāraṁ ca me bahu poṣitavyamiti |
bhagavānāha—-yadi mūlyaṁ dīyate, pratimuñcasīti? |
Vai 085.026 sa kathayati—pratimokṣyāmi bhagavanniti |
atha bhagavāmllaukikacittamutpādayati—aho bata śakro devendrastrīṇi kārṣāpaṇasahasrāṇyādāyāgacchediti |
Vai 085.028 sahacittotpādādbhagavataḥ śakro devendraḥ kārṣāpaṇasahasratrayamādāya bhagavataḥ purastādasthāt |
Vai 085.029 atha bhagavāñ śakraṁ devendramidamavocat—anuprayaccha kauśika asya goghātakasya triguṇaṁ mūlyam |
Vai 085.030 adācchakro devendrastasya goghātakasya kārṣāpaṇatrayasahasraṁ vṛṣamūlyam |
atha goghātakaḥ kārṣāpaṇasahasrayaṁ vṛṣamūlyaṁ gṛhītvā hṛṣṭastuṣṭaḥ pramudito bhagavataḥ pādau śirasā vanditvā taṁ govṛṣaṁ bandhanānmuktvā prakrāntaḥ |
Vai 085.032 śakro devendro bhagavataḥ pādau śirasā vanditvā tatraivāntarhitaḥ |

Vai 86.000atha govṛṣo gatapratyāgataprāṇo bhūyasyā mātrayā bhagavatyabhiprasanno bhagavantaṁ triḥ pradakṣiṇīkṛtya pṛṣṭhataḥ pṛṣṭhataḥ samanubaddho bhagavato mukhaṁ vyavalokayamāno ’sthāt |
Vai 086.002 atha bhagavān smitamakārṣīt |
Vai 086.003 dharmatā khalu yasmin samaye buddhā bhagavantaḥ smitaṁ prāviṣkurvanti, tasmin samaye nīlapītalohitāvadātāḥ puṣpaparāgapadmarāgavajravaiḍūryamuasāragalvārkalohitakādakṣiṇāvartaśaṅkhaśilāpravālajātarūparajatavarṇā arciṣo mukhānniścārya kāścidadhastādgacchanti, kāścidupariṣṭādgacchanti |
Vai 086.006 yā adhastādgacchanti, tāḥ saṁjīvaṁ kālasūtraṁ rauravaṁ mahārauravaṁ tapanaṁ pratāpanamavīcimarbudaṁ nirarbudamaṭaṭaṁ hahavaṁ huhuvamutpalaṁ padmaṁ mahāpadmaparyantān narakān gatvā ye uṣṇanarakāsteṣu śītībhūtvā nipatanti, ye śītanarakāsteṣūṣṇībhūtvā nipatanti |
Vai 086.008 tena teṣāṁ sattvānāṁ kāraṇāviśeṣāḥ pratiprasrabhyante |
Vai 086.009 teṣāmevaṁ bhavati—kiṁ nu vayaṁ bhavanta itaścyutāḥ, āhosvidanyatropapannā iti |
teṣāṁ prasādasaṁjananārthaṁ bhagavān nirmitaṁ visarjayati |
Vai 086.010 teṣāṁ nirmitaṁ dṛṣṭvā evaṁ bhavati—na hyeva vayaṁ bhavanta itaścyutāḥ, nāpyanyatropapannā iti |
Vai 086.011 api tvayamapūrvadarśanaḥ sattvaḥ, asyānubhāvenāsmākaṁ kāraṇāviśeṣāḥ pratiprasrabdhā iti |
Vai 086.012 te nirmite cittamabhiprasādya tannarakavedanīyaṁ karma kṣapayitvā devamanuṣyeṣu pratisaṁdhir gṛhṇanti yatra satyānāṁ bhājanabhūtā bhavanti |
Vai 086.013 yā upariṣṭādgacchanti, tāścāturmahārājakāyikān devāṁstrāyastriṁśān yāmāṁstuṣitān nirmāṇaratīn paranirmitavaśavartino brahmakāyikān brahmapurohitān mahābrahmaṇaḥ parīttābhānapramāṇābhānābhāsvarān parīttaśubhānapramāṇaśubhāñ śubhakṛtsnānanabhrakān puṇyaprasavān bṛhatphalānabṛhānatapān sudṛśān sudarśanānakaniṣṭhaparyantān devān gatvā anityaṁ duḥkhaṁ śūnyamanātmetyuddhoṣayanti |
Vai 086.017 gāthādvayaṁ bhāṣante—Vai 086.018 ārabhadhvaṁ niṣkrāmata yujyadhvaṁ buddhaśāsane |
Vai 086.019 dhunīta mṛtyunaḥ sainyaṁ naḍāgāramiva kuñjaraḥ (1)|
Vai 086.020 yo hyasmin dharmavinaye apramattaścariṣyati |
Vai 086.021 prahāya jātisaṁsāraṁ duḥkhasyāntaṁ kariṣyati (2)|
iti |
Vai 086.022 atha tā arciṣastrisāhasramahāsāhasraṁ lokadhātumanvāhiṇḍya bhagavantameva pṛṣṭhataḥ pṛṣṭhataḥ samanugacchanti |
Vai 086.023 tadyadi bhagavānatītaṁ karma vyākartukāmo bhavati, bhagavataḥ pṛṣṭhato ’ntardhīyante |
Vai 086.024 anāgataṁ vyākartukāmo bhavati, purastādantardhīyante |
narakopapattiṁ vyākartukāmo bhavati, pādatale-ntardhīyante |
Vai 086.025 tiryagupapattiṁ vyākartukāmo bhavati, pārṣṇyāmantardhīyante |
pretopapattiṁ vyākartukāmo bhavati, pādāṅguṣṭhe ’ntardhīyante |
Vai 086.026 manuṣyopapattiṁ vyākartukāmo bhavati, jānunorantardhīyante |
Vai 086.027 balacakravartirājyaṁ vyākartukāmo bhavati, vāme karatale ’ntardhīyante |
cakravartirājyaṁ vyākartukāmo bhavati, dakṣiṇe karatale ’ntardhīyante |
Vai 086.028 devopapattiṁ vyākartukāmo bhavati, nābhyāmantardhīyante |
Vai 086.029 śrāvakabodhiṁ vyākartukāmo bhavati, āsye ’ntardhīyante |
pratyekāṁ bodhiṁ vyākartukāmo bhavati, ūrṇāyāmantardhīyante |
Vai 086.030 anuttarāṁ samyaksambodhiṁ vyākartukāmo bhavati, uṣṇīṣe ’ntardhīyante |
Vai 086.031 atha tā arciṣo bhagavantaṁ triḥ pradakṣiṇīkṛtya bhagavata ūrṇāyāmantarhitāḥ |

Vai 087.001 Vai 87.000avabhāsitā yena diśaḥ samantāddivākareṇodayatā yathaiva (3)|
Vai 087.003 gāthāṁ ca bhāṣite—Vai 087.004 vigatodbhavā dainyamadaprahīṇā buddhā jagatyuttamahetubhūtāḥ |
Vai 087.006 nākāraṇaṁ śaṅkhamṛṇālagauraṁ smitamupadarśayanti jinā jitārayaḥ (4)|
Vai 087.008 tatkālaṁ svayamadhigamya dhīra buddhyā śrotṛrṇāṁ śramaṇa jinendra kāṅkṣitānām |
Vai 087.010 dhīrābhirmunivṛṣa vāgbhiruttamābhirutpannaṁ vyapanaya saṁśayaṁ śubhābhiḥ (5)|

Vai 087.016 bhagavānāha—evametadānanda, evametat |
nāhetvapratyayamānanda tathāgatā arhantaḥ samyaksambuddhāḥ smitaṁ prāviṣkurvanti |
Vai 087.017 dṛṣṭaste ānanda ayaṁ govṛṣah? dṛṣṭo bhadanta |
eṣa ānanda govṛṣastathāgatasyāntike prasannacittaḥ saptame divase kālaṁ kṛtvā cātrumahārājikeṣu deveṣūpapatsyate |
Vai 087.019 vaiśravaṇasya mahārājasya putro bhaviṣyati |
tataścyutvā trāyastriṁśeṣu deveṣūpapatsyate |
Vai 087.020 śakrasya devendrasya putro bhaviṣyati |
tataścyutvā yāmeṣu deveṣūpapatsyate |
yāmasya devasya putro bhaviṣyati |
Vai 087.021 tataścyutvā tuṣiteṣu deveṣūpapatsyate |
sa tuṣitasya devasya putro bhaviṣyati |
Vai 087.022 tataścyutvā nirmāṇaratiṣu deveṣūpapatsyate |
sunirmitasya devaputrasya putro bhaviṣyati |
tataścyutvā parinirmitavaśavartiṣu deveṣūpapatsyate |
Vai 087.023 vaśavaratino devaputrasya putro bhaviṣyati |
tadanayā saṁtatyā navanavatikalpasahasrāṇi vinipātaṁ na gamiṣyati |
Vai 087.024 tataḥ kāmāvacareṣu deveṣu divyaṁ sukhamanubhūya paścime bhave paścime nikete samucchraye paścime ātmabhāvapratilambhe manuṣyatvaṁ pratilabhya rājā bhaviṣyati aśokavarṇo nāma cakravartī caturarṇavāntavijetā dhārmiko dharmarājaḥ saptaratnasamanvāgataḥ |
Vai 087.027 tasyemānyevamrūpāṇi sapta ratnāni bhaviṣyanti |
tadyathā—cakraratnaṁ hastiratnamaśvaratnaṁ maṇiratnaṁ strīratnaṁ gṛhapatiratnaṁ pariṇāyakaratnameva saptamam |
Vai 087.028 pūrṇaṁ cāsya bhaviṣyati sahasraṁ putrāṇāṁ śūrāṇāṁ vīrāṇāṁ varāṅgarūpiṇā parasainyapramardakānām |
Vai 087.029 imāmeva samudraparyantāṁ mahāpṛthivīmakhilāmakaṇṭakāmanutpīḍāmadaṇḍenāśastreṇa dharmyeṇa samayenābhinirjitya adhyāvatsyati |
Vai 087.031 so ’pareṇa samayena dānāni datvā cakravartirājyamapahāya keśaśmaśrūṇyavatārya kāṣāyāṇi vastrāṇi …ṣamyageva śraddhayā agārādanagārikāṁ pravrajya pratyekāṁ bodhiṁ sākṣātkariṣyati, aśokavarṇo nāma pratyekabuddho bhaviṣyati |
Vai 087.033 athāyuṣmānānandaḥ kṛtakarapuṭo bhagavantaṁ papraccha—kiṁ bhadanta anena govṛṣeṇa karma kṛtam yena tiryagyonāvupapannaḥ, kiṁ karma kṛtam yena divyamānuṣasukhamanubhūya Vai 88.000pratyekāṁ bodhimadhigamiṣyati? bhagavānāha—anenaiva ānanda govṛṣeṇa karmāṇi kṛtānyupacitāni labdhasambhārāṇi pariṇatapratyayāni oghavatpratyupasthitānyavaśyambhāvīni |
Vai 088.002 govṛṣeṇa karmāṇi kṛtānyupacitāni |
Vai 088.003 ko ’nyaḥ pratyanubhaviṣyati? na hyānanda karmāṇi kṛtānyupacitāni bāhye pṛthivīdhātau vipacyante, nābdhātau, na tejodhātau, na vāyudhātau, api tūpātteṣveva skandhadhātvāyataneṣu karmāṇi kṛtāni vipacyante śubhāśubhāni ca |
