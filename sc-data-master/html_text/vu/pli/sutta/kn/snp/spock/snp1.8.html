Metta Sutta

Wuh buddha's zhit fi' loving kindness

Nash tor ra dang nam-tor ovsoh
by veh vi tor skilled svi' goodness,
heh vi fai-tor wuh yut t' sochya:
let au nam-tor kup heh abruvenik,
straightforward heh gentle svi' starun,
humble heh ri conceited,
contented heh easily satisfied,
unburdened k' gu-vam heh frugal svi' ish-veh yut.
peaceful heh hayal heh kau-bosh heh skillful,
not yaut il demanding svi' malat.
kal-tor au ri tor wuh slightest vel
that wuh kau-bosh would later reprove.
wishing: svi' gladness heh svi' ek'manek,
may ek' beings nam-tor na' ease.
whatever living beings tra' lau nam-tor;
whether au nam-tor kobat il karik, omitting rim,
wuh lo'uk il wuh mighty, medium, pen il pi',
the gla-tor heh wuh unseen,
eifa living be' heh irak sad,
those pukeshta heh to-be-born â€”
lau ek' beings nam-tor na' ease!

Nash tor ra dang nam-tor ovsoh
by veh vi tor skilled svi' goodness,
heh vi fai-tor wuh yut t' sochya:
let au nam-tor kup heh abruvenik,
kal-tor rim tehs-tor wuh'ashiv,
or despise fan hasu svi' fan fupa s'.
kal-tor rim k'fai reshan il ill-will
wish klau fi-tor wuh'ashiv.
kauk u' wuh ko-mekh protects k' ish-veh ha'kiv
her kan, ish-veh goh kan,
ni k' wuh boundless khaf-spol
should veh tishau ek' living beings;
radiating kindness abru' wuh entire panu:
spreading abru'le tor wuh igen,
heh ne'le tor wuh gluder;
outwards heh unbounded,
el-tor s' hatred heh ill-will.
whether standing il walking, hayal-tor il lying ne'
elik s' drowsiness,
one dang potun-tor nash recollection.
nash tor tar-tor tor nam-tor wuh sublime abiding.
by ri holding tor teslau zan-tor,
wuh pure-hearted veh, having starpa'shaya t' vision,
being el-tor s' ek' kash-ral aitlu,
nam-tor ri pukeshta va'ashiv vi' nash panu
