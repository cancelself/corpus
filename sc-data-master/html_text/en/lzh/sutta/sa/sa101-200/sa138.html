• Saṁyuktāgama

138. [Fourth Discourse on Abandoning Doubt]

A third discourse is also like this, with the difference: “He abandons doubt in relation to the Buddha, in relation to the Dharma, in relation to the Community, and he abandons doubt in relation to dukkha, in relation to its arising, in relation to its cessation, and in relation to the path.”

Translated by Bhikkhu Anālayo. Originally published in ‘On Rādha and Views – A Translation of Saṁyukta-āgama Discourses 111 to 138 (Fascicle 6)’, Dharma Drum Journal of Buddhist Studies, 2015, vol. 17 pp. 1–42.
