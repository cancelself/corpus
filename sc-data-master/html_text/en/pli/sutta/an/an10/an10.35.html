10.35. Dependence

SC 1“Bhante, how many qualities should a bhikkhu possess to give dependence?”

SC 2“A bhikkhu who possesses ten qualities, Upāli, may give dependence. What ten?” …

The same ten qualities as in the preceding sutta.

SC 3“A bhikkhu who possesses these ten qualities may give dependence.”
