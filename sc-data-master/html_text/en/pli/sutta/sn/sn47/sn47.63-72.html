47.63–72. The Tathagata, Etc.

SC 1(To be elaborated by way of the establishments of mindfulness parallel to 45:139-48.)

SC 2Tathagata, footprint, roof peak,
Roots, heartwood, jasmine,
Monarch, the moon and sun,
Together with the cloth as tenth.
