liberated from all sorrows. Verse 166

Walt 3541 Giving scissors which were slender
and very sharp too for the monks,
I get the knowledge, unmatched and
pure, which cuts off the defilements. Verse 167

Walt 3542 Giving tweezers for the Buddha
and the monks of splendid virtue,
I get the knowledge, unmatched and
pure, which pulls out the defilements. Verse 168

Walt 3543 Giving nose-cleaners for Buddha
and the monks of splendid virtue,
I experience eight results,
in accordance with my karma. Verse 169

Walt 3544 I have faith and morality,
shame, the virtue of fearing sin,
I’m happy, generous, patient,
and wisdom is my eighth virtue. Verse 170

Walt 3545 Giving short chairs for the Buddha
and the monks of splendid virtue,
I experience five results,
in accordance with my karma. Verse 171

Walt 3546 I’m born in an eminent clan,
I become a very rich man,
everyone is honoring me,
glory is arising for me. Verse 172

Walt 3547 For one hundred thousand aeons
palanquins atop four horses
are constantly waiting on me,
enjoying giving them away. Verse 173

Walt 3548 Giving cushions for the Buddha
and the monks of splendid virtue,
I experience six results,
in accordance with my karma. Verse 174

Walt 3549 My body is proportional,
I’m honored, gentle, good-looking,
I get an entourage that’s smart:
that’s the fruit of giving cushions. Verse 175

Walt 3550 Cotton mattresses and varied
coverlets of wool and of silk;
I receive various sorts of
fine hemp cloth and woolen blankets. Verse 176

Walt 3551 And cloaks which are soft on the skin,
and soft leather and bamboo ones;
I am receiving varied rugs:
that’s the fruit of giving cushions. Verse 177

Walt 3552 As far back as I remember,
ever since I reached discretion,
not vain, meditation’s my bed:
that’s the fruit of giving cushions. Verse 178

Walt 3553 Giving pillows for the Victor
and the monks of splendid virtue,
I experience six results,
in accordance with my karma. Verse 179

Walt 3554 I am being cushioned upon
pillows made of wool and lotus
and also of red sandalwood;
I have an outstanding body. Verse 180

Walt 3555 The excellent Eightfold Path and
the four fruits that come from monkhood:
knowledge of these is brought to me
and I dwell there all of the time. Verse 181

Walt 3556 Giving, taming, and self-control;
and the four forms of boundlessness:
knowledge of these is brought to me
and I dwell there all of the time. Verse 182

Walt 3557 Vows and virtues and practices,
also the types of good manners:
knowledge of these is brought to me
and I dwell there every day. Verse 183

Walt 3558 Walking back and forth or striving;
exertion which leads to wisdom:
knowledge of these is brought to me;
I dwell according to my wish. Verse 184

Walt 3559 Morality, meditation
and wisdom, unsurpassed freedom:
knowledge of these is brought to me
and I dwell full of happiness. Verse 185

Walt 3560 Giving straw stools for the Victor
and the monks of splendid virtue,
I experience four results,
in accordance with my karma. Verse 186

Walt 3561 I’m finding the best palanquins,
made of gold and made of gemstones,
made of ivory and fine wood:
that’s the fruit of stools made of straw. Verse 187

Walt 3562 Giving footstools for the Victor
and the monks of splendid virtue,
I experience two results,
in accordance with my karma. Verse 188

Walt 3563I receive many vehicles:
that is the fruit of a footstool.
Slave-girls and slaves and also wives
and all my other dependents
do properly look after me:
that is the fruit of a footstool. Verse 189

Walt 3564 Giving oils for anointing
for Buddha and the supreme group,
I experience five results,
in accordance with my karma. Verse 190

Walt 3565 Lack of illness, having beauty,
and quickly grasping the Teaching,
receiving of much food and drink,
and long life is the fifth for me. Verse 191

Walt 3566 Giving clarified butter for
monks of supreme, splendid virtue,
I experience five results,
in accordance with my karma. Verse 192

Walt 3567 I am strong, endowed with beauty,
always happy and born slender.
I am free of disease, and pure:
that is the fruit of giving ghee. Verse 193

Walt 3568 Giving cleaners for the mouth for
the Buddha and the supreme group,
I experience five results,
in accordance with my karma. Verse 194

Walt 3569 I have a clean throat and sweet sound,
I am free of coughs of asthma,
and the scent of blue lotuses
is always wafting from my mouth. Verse 195

Walt 3570 Giving well-prepared milk-curds for
the Buddha and the supreme group,
I’m enjoying ambrosial things
and mindfulness of my body. Verse 196

Walt 3571 Giving honey with color, scent
and taste for Buddha and the group,
I now drink the juice of freedom,
which is unmatched, beyond compare. Verse 197

Walt 3572 Giving actual juice for the
Buddha and for the supreme group,
I experience the four fruits,
in accordance with my karma. Verse 198

Walt 3573 Having given food and drink for
the Buddha and the supreme group
I experience ten results,
in accordance with my karma. Verse 199

Walt 3574 I am always long-lived and strong,
a hero, beautiful, famous
and happy; a recipient
of food and drink, courageous, smart.
As I transmigrate in the world,
I am receiving these virtues. Verse 200

Walt 3575 Giving incense for the Buddha
and the monks of splendid virtue,
I experience ten results,
in accordance with my karma. Verse 201

Walt 3576 My body smells good, I’m famous,
quick-thinking as well as clever;
I have sharp, extensive wisdom;
I have bright and deep wisdom. Verse 202

Walt 3577 As I transmigrate in the world,
I have wide and alert wisdom.
Now, because of giving that, I’ve
attained auspicious, happy peace. Verse 203

Walt 3578 My being in Buddha’s presence
was a very good thing for me.
The three knowledges are attained;
I have done what the Buddha taught! Verse 204

Walt 3579 My defilements are now burnt up;
all new existence is destroyed.
Like elephants with broken chains,
I am living without constraint. Verse 205

Walt 3580 The four analytical modes,
and these eight deliverances,
six special knowledges mastered,
I have done what the Buddha taught! Verse 206

Thus indeed Venerable Pilindavaccha Thera spoke these verses.

The legend of Pilindavaccha Thera is finished.
