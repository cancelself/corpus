381. {384.} Pilakkhaphaladāyaka

Walt 3298 Seeing Buddha in the forest,
Atthadassi, Greatly Famed One,
happy, with pleasure in my heart,
I gave wave-leafed fig fruit to him. Verse 1

Walt 3299 In the eighteen hundred aeons
since I gave fruit to the Buddha,
I’ve come to know no bad rebirth:
that is the fruit of giving fruit. Verse 2

Walt 3300 Being in Best Buddha’s presence
was a very good thing for me.
The three knowledges are attained;
I have done what the Buddha taught! Verse 3

Walt 3301 My defilements are now burnt up;
all new existence is destroyed.
Like elephants with broken chains,
I am living without constraint. Verse 4

Walt 3302 The four analytical modes,
and these eight deliverances,
six special knowledges mastered,
I have done what the Buddha taught! Verse 5

Thus indeed Venerable Pilakkhaphaladāyaka Thera spoke these verses.

The legend of Pilakkhaphaladāyaka Thera is finished.

Translated by Jonathan S. Walters.
