6.7. Gutta

SC 1SC Verse 163PTS Verse 163Ms 19Th2_224Msdiv 163[The Buddha admonished me:]
Gutta, devote yourself to the goal
for which you went forth,
having discarded [hope]
for a dear son of your own.
Don't fall under the sway
of the mind.

SC 2SC Verse 164PTS Verse 164Ms 19Th2_225Msdiv 164Hoodwinked by mind,
beings in love with Mara's realm,
roam
through the many-birth wandering-on,
unknowing.

SC 3SC Verse 165PTS Verse 165Ms 19Th2_226Msdiv 165Abandoning these lower fetters, nun —
sensual desire, ill will,
self-identity views,

SC 4SC Verse 166PTS Verse 166Ms 19Th2_227Msdiv 166grasping at precepts & practices,
and uncertainty as the fifth —
you won't come
to this again.

SC 5SC Verse 167PTS Verse 167Ms 19Th2_228Msdiv 167Forsaking passion, conceit,
ignorance, & restlessness
—cutting through [all] the fetters —
you will make an end
of suffering & stress.

SC 6SC Verse 168PTS Verse 168Ms 19Th2_229Msdiv 168Discarding birth & wandering-on,
comprehending further becoming,
free from hunger
in the right-here-&-now
you will go about
totally calmed.
