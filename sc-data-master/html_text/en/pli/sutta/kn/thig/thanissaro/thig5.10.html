5.10. Patacara

SC 1SC Verse 112PTS Verse 112Ms 19Th2_163Msdiv 112[I thought:]
"Plowing the field with plows,
sowing the ground with seed,
supporting their wives & children,
young men gather up wealth.

SC 2SC Verse 113PTS Verse 113Ms 19Th2_164Msdiv 113So why is it that I,
consummate in virtue,
a doer of the teacher's bidding,
don't gain Unbinding?
I'm not lazy or proud."

SC 3SC Verse 114PTS Verse 114Ms 19Th2_165Msdiv 114Washing my feet, I noticed
the water.
And in watching it flow from high
to low,

SC 4SC Verse 115PTS Verse 115PTS vp Pali 135Ms 19Th2_166Msdiv 115my heart was composed
like a fine thoroughbred steed.
Then taking a lamp, I entered the hut,
checked the bedding,
sat down on the bed.

SC 5SC Verse 116PTS Verse 116Ms 19Th2_167Msdiv 116And taking a pin, I pulled out the wick:
Like the flame's unbinding
was the liberation
of awareness.
