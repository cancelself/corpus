The Ten Training Precepts

  1. SC 1I undertake to abide by the precept to abstain from killing.
  2. SC 2I undertake to abide by the precept to abstain from stealing.
  3. SC 3I undertake to abide by the precept to abstain from sexual misconduct.
  4. SC 4I undertake to abide by the precept to abstain from lying.
  5. SC 5I undertake to abide by the precept to abstain from liquor that causes intoxication and heedlessness.
  6. SC 6I undertake to abide by the precept to abstain from untimely eating.
  7. SC 7I undertake to abide by the precept to abstain from dancing, singing, music, and visiting unseemly shows.
  8. SC 8I undertake to abide by the precept to abstain from the use of garlands, perfumes, cosmetics, and embellishments.
  9. SC 9I undertake to abide by the precept to abstain from the use of high and luxurious beds.
  10. SC 10I undertake to abide by the precept to abstain from accepting gold and silver.

Translated by Piyadassi Thera.
