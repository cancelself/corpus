The Ten Training Rules

SC 1I undertake the training rule of refraining from killing living creatures

SC 2I undertake the training rule of refraining from taking what has not been given

SC 3I undertake the training rule of refraining from unchastity

SC 4I undertake the training rule of refraining from false speech

SC 5I undertake the training rule of refraining from liquor, wines, or intoxicants which cause heedlessness.

SC 6I undertake the training rule of refraining from eating at the wrong time

SC 7I undertake the training rule of refraining from dances, songs, music, and watching shows

SC 8I undertake the training rule of refraining from adorning or ornamenting myself by wearing garlands, scents, or ointments

SC 9I undertake the training rule of refraining from lofty or grand beds

SC 10I undertake the training rule of refraining from accepting gold or money

Translated by Bhikkhu Ānandajoti.
