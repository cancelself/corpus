• Short Readings 4

The Questions to the Boy

SC 1What is said to be one?

All beings subsist on food.

SC 2What is said to be two?

Mind and body.

SC 3What is said to be three?

The three feelings.

SC 4What is said to be four?

The four noble truths.

SC 5What is said to be five?

The five constituent groups of mind and body that provide fuel for attachment.

SC 6What is said to be six?

The six internal sense spheres.

SC 7What is said to be seven?

The seven factors of Awakening.

SC 8What is said to be eight?

The noble path with eight factors.

SC 9What is said to be nine?

The nine abodes of beings.

SC 10What is said to be ten?

When endowed with ten factors he is said to be Worthy.

Translated by Bhikkhu Ānandajoti.
