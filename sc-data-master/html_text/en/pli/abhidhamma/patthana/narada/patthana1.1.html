Analytical Exposition of the Conditions

1. Root Condition

Pts-vp-en 2 The roots are related to the states which are associated with roots, and the matter produced thereby, by root condition.

2. Object Condition

  1. Visible object-base is related to eye-consciousness element and its associated states by object condition.
  2. Sound-base is related to ear-consciousness element and its associated states by object condition.
  3. Odour-base is related to nose-consciousness element and its associated states by object condition.
  4. Taste-base is related to tongue-consciousness element and its associated states by object condition.
  5. Tangible object-base is related to body-consciousness element and its associated states by object condition.
  6. Visible object-base, sound-base, odour-base, taste-base, tangible object-base is related to mind-element and its associated states by object condition.
  7. All states are related to mind-consciousness element and its associated states by object condition.
  8. Taking any state as object, these states, consciousness and mental factors, arise; those (former) states are related to those (latter) states by object condition.

3. Predominance Condition

  1. Predominant desire is related to the states associated with desire, and the matter produced thereby, by predominance condition.
  2. Predominant effort is related to the states associated with effort, and the matter produced thereby, by predominance condition.
  3. Predominant consciousness is related to the states associated with consciousness, and the matter produced thereby, by predominance condition.
  4. Predominant investigating-wisdom is related to the Pts-vp-en 3 | states associated with investigating-wisdom, and the matter produced thereby, by predominance condition.
  5. Taking any state as estimable object, these states, consciousness and mental factors, arise; those (former) states are related to those (latter) states by predominance condition.

4. Proximity Condition

  1. Eye-consciousness element and its associated states are related to mind-element and its associated states by proximity condition; mind-element and its associated states are related to mind-consciousness element and its associated states by proximity condition.
  2. Ear-consciousness element and its associated states are related to mind-element and its associated states by proximity condition; mind-element and its associated states are related to mind-consciousness element and its associated states by proximity condition.
  3. Nose-consciousness element and its associated states are related to mind-element and its associated states by proximity condition; mind-element and its associated states are related to mind-consciousness element and its associated states by proximity condition.
  4. Tongue-consciousness element and its associated states are related to mind-element and its associated states by proximity condition; mind-element and its associated states are related to mind-consciousness element and its associated states by proximity condition.
  5. Body-consciousness element and its associated states are related to mind-element and its associated states by proximity condition; mind-element and its associated states are related to mind-consciousness element and its associated states by proximity condition.
  6. Preceding faultless states are related to subsequent faultless states by proximity condition.
  7. Preceding faultless states are related to subsequent indeterminate states by proximity condition.
  8. Preceding faulty states are related to subsequent faulty states by proximity condition.
  9. Pts-vp-en 4 Preceding faulty states are related to subsequent indeterminate states by proximity condition.
  10. Preceding indeterminate states are related to subsequent indeterminate states by proximity condition.
  11. Preceding indeterminate states are related to subsequent faultless states by proximity condition.
  12. Preceding indeterminate states are related to subsequent faulty states by proximity condition.
  13. In proximity to these states, these (other) states, consciousness and mental factors, arise; those (former) states are related to those (latter) states by proximity condition.

5. Contiguity Condition

  1. Eye-consciousness element and its associated states are related to mind-element and its associated states by contiguity condition; mind-element and its associated states are related to mind-consciousness element and its associated states by contiguity condition.
  2. Ear-consciousness element and its associated states are related to mind-element and its associated states by contiguity condition; mind-element and its associated states are related to mind-consciousness element and its associated states by contiguity condition.
  3. Nose-consciousness element and its associated states are related to mind-element and its associated states by contiguity condition; mind-element and its associated states are related to mind-consciousness element and its associated states by contiguity condition.
  4. Tongue-consciousness element and its associated states are related to mind-element and its associated states by contiguity condition; mind-element and its associated states are related to mind-consciousness element and its associated states by contiguity condition.
  5. Body-consciousness element and its associated states are related to mind-element and its associated states by contiguity condition; mind-element and its associated states are related to mind-consciousness element and its associated states by contiguity condition.
  6. Pts-vp-en 5 Preceding faultless states are related to subsequent faultless states by contiguity condition.
  7. Preceding faultless states are related to subsequent indeterminate states by contiguity condition.
  8. Preceding faulty states are related to subsequent faulty states by contiguity condition.
  9. Preceding faulty states are related to subsequent indeterminate states by contiguity condition.
  10. Preceding indeterminate states are related to subsequent indeterminate states by contiguity condition.
  11. Preceding indeterminate states are related to subsequent faultless states by contiguity condition.
  12. Preceding indeterminate states are related to subsequent faulty states by contiguity condition.
  13. In contiguity with Samanantarā these states, these (other) states, consciousness and mental factors, arise; those (former) states are related to those (latter) states by contiguity condition.

6. Conascence Condition

  1. The four immaterial (i.e. mental) aggregates are mutually related to one another by conascence condition.
  2. The four great primaries are mutually related to one another by conascence condition.
  3. At the moment of conception, mentality and materiality are mutually related to one another by conascence condition.
  4. States, consciousness and mental factors, are related to mind-produced matter by conascence condition.
  5. The great primaries are related to derived matter by conascence condition.
  6. The material states are sometimes related to the immaterial states by conascence condition and are sometimes not related by conascence condition.

7. Mutuality Condition

  1. The four immaterial aggregates are related to one another by mutuality condition.
  2. The four great primaries are related to one another by mutuality condition.
  3. Pts-vp-en 6 At the moment of conception, mentality and materiality are related to one another by mutuality condition.

8. Dependence Condition

  1. The four immaterial aggregates are mutually related to one another by dependence condition.
  2. The four great primaries are mutually related to one another by dependence condition.
  3. At the moment of conception, mentality and materiality are mutually related to one another by dependence condition.
  4. States, consciousness and mental factors, are related to mind-produced matter by dependence condition.
  5. The great primaries are related to derived matter by dependence condition.
  6. Eye-base is related to eye-consciousness element and its associated states by dependence condition.
  7. Ear-base is related to ear-consciousness element and its associated states by dependence condition.
  8. Nose-base is related to nose-consciousness element and its associated states by dependence condition.
  9. Tongue-base is related to tongue-consciousness element and its associated states by dependence condition.
  10. Body-base is related to body-consciousness element and its associated states by dependence condition.
  11. Depending on this matter (i.e. heart-base), mind-element and mind-consciousness element arise; that matter is related to mind-element and mind-consciousness element and their associated states by dependence condition.

9. Strong-Dependence Condition

  1. Preceding faultless states are related to subsequent faultless states by strong-dependence condition.
  2. Preceding faultless states are related to some subsequent faulty states by strong-dependence condition.
  3. Preceding faultless states are related to subsequent indeterminate states by strong-dependence condition.
  4. Preceding faulty states are related to subsequent faulty states by strong-dependence condition.
  5. Pts-vp-en 7 Preceding faulty states are related to some subsequent faultless states by strong-dependence condition.
  6. Preceding faulty states are related to subsequent indeterminate states by strong-dependence condition.
  7. Preceding indeterminate states are related to subsequent indeterminate states by strong-dependence condition.
  8. Preceding indeterminate states are related to subsequent faultless states by strong-dependence condition.
  9. Preceding indeterminate states are related to subsequent faulty states by strong-dependence condition.
  10. Also, weather and food are related by strong-dependence condition; a person is related by strong-dependence condition; a lodging-place is related by strong-dependence condition.

10. Prenascence Condition

  1. Eye-base is related to eye-consciousness element and its associated states by prenascence condition.
  2. Ear-base is related to ear-consciousness element and its associated states by prenascence condition.
  3. Nose-base is related to nose-consciousness element and its associated states by prenascence condition.
  4. Tongue-base is related to tongue-consciousness element and its associated states by prenascence condition.
  5. Body-base is related to body-consciousness element and its associated states by prenascence condition.
  6. Visible object-base is related to eye-consciousness element and its associated states by prenascence condition.
  7. Sound-base is related to ear-consciousness element and its associated states by prenascence condition.
  8. Odour-base is related to nose-consciousness element and its associated states by prenascence condition.
  9. Taste-base is related to tongue-consciousness element and its associated states by prenascence condition.
  10. Tangible object-base is related to body-consciousness element and its associated states by prenascence condition.
  11. Visible object-base, sound-base, odour-base, taste-base, tangible object-base is related to mind-element and its associated states by prenascence condition.
  12. Pts-vp-en 8 Depending on this matter, mind-element and mind-consciousness element arise; that matter is related to mind-element and its associated states by prenascence condition; is sometimes related to mind-consciousness element and its associated states by prenascence condition, and is sometimes not related by prenascence condition.

11. Postnascence Condition

The states, postnascent consciousness and mental factors, are related to this prenascent body by postnascence condition.

12. Repetition Condition

  1. Preceding faultless states are related to subsequent faultless states by repetition condition.
  2. Preceding faulty states are related to subsequent faulty states by repetition condition.
  3. Preceding functional indeterminate states are related to subsequent functional indeterminate states by repetition condition.

13. Kamma Condition

  1. Faultless and faulty kamma is related to resultant aggregates and kamma-produced matter by kamma condition.
  2. Volition is related to its associated states, and the matter produced thereby, by kamma condition.

14. Resultant Condition

The four immaterial resultant aggregates are mutually related to one another by resultant condition.

15. Nutriment Condition

  1. Edible food is related to this body by nutriment condition.
  2. The immaterial nutriments are related to their associated states, and the matter produced thereby, by nutriment condition.

16. Faculty Condition

  1. Pts-vp-en 9 Eye-faculty is related to eye-consciousness element and its associated states by faculty condition.
  2. Ear-faculty is related to ear-consciousness element and its associated states by faculty condition.
  3. Nose-faculty is related to nose-consciousness element and its associated states by faculty condition.
  4. Tongue-faculty is related to tongue-consciousness element and its associated states by faculty condition.
  5. Body-faculty is related to body-consciousness element and its associated states by faculty condition.
  6. Physical life-faculty is related to kamma-produced matter by faculty condition.
  7. The immaterial faculties are related to their associated states, and the matter produced thereby, by faculty condition.

17. Jhāna Condition

The jhāna factors are related to their associated states, and the matter produced thereby, by jhāna condition.

18. Path Condition

The path factors are related to their associated states, and the matter produced thereby, by path condition.

19. Association Condition

The four immaterial aggregates are mutually related to one another by association condition.

20. Dissociation Condition

  1. The material states are related to the immaterial states by dissociation condition.
  2. The immaterial states are related to the material states by dissociation condition.

  5. The great primaries are related to derived matter by presence condition.
  6. Eye-base is related to eye-consciousness element and its associated states by presence condition.

  8. Nose-base is related to nose-consciousness element and its associated states by presence condition.
  9. Tongue-base is related to tongue-consciousness element and its associated states by presence condition.
  10. Body-base is related to body-consciousness element and its associated states by presence condition.

22. Absence Condition

States, consciousness and mental factors, which have just ceased in contiguity, are related to the present states, consciousness and mental factors, by absence condition.

23. Disappearance Condition
