8.2.2. Specification of Consciousness Mixing by Mean of Suttanta (Suttantacittanissakavisesa)

PTS vp En 8.114Mind with greed arises, and does not cease at this person. Mind with greed will cease, and will not arise at that person? At the rising moment of the last mind with greed, mind with greed arises, does not cease, will cease, and will not arise at those persons. At other persons, at the rising moment of mind with greed, mind with greed … pe …
