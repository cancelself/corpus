• 6. Pairs on Formations (Saṅkhāra Yamaka)

6.3. Chapter on Comprehension (Paññāvāra)

PTS vp En 6.159This person comprehends body formation. Does that person comprehend verbal formation? Yes.

This person comprehends verbal formation. Does that person comprehend body formation? Yes.

(As the Chapter on Comprehension at Khandha Yamaka is classified, so should this Chapter on Comprehension at Saṅkhāra Yamaka be classified.)

End of Chapter on Comprehension.

End of Pairs on Formations.
