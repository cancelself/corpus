5.3.3. Chapter on the Future (Anagatavāra)

PTS vp En 5.167This person will comprehended suffering-truth. Will that person eradicate origination-truth? Yes.

Or else, this person … pe … Yes.

This person will not comprehended suffering-truth. Will that person not eradicate origination-truth? Yes.

Or else, this person … pe … Yes.
