17.8 Of the Order and Daily Life

Controverted Point: That it should not be said that “`The Order `enjoys', `eats', `drinks”'.

The reason and the adherents as above.

Theravādin: PTS cs 17.8.1But you must admit that there are those who partake of the meals of the Order, both daily and on special occasions, both of rice-gruel and of drink.

PTS cs 17.8.2Moreover, did not the Exalted One speak of “meals taken in company”, “in turn”, “of food left over”, and “not left over”? PTS cs 17.8.3 And did He not speak of eight kinds of drinks: “mango-syrup, jambu-syrup, plantain-syrup, mocha-syrup, honey-syrup, grape-juice, lilyroot-syrup, and phārusaka-syrup”? How then can you maintain your view?

Mahāsuññatāvādin: PTS cs 17.8.4But does a Path, does Fruition “enjoy”, “eat”, “drink”? … .
