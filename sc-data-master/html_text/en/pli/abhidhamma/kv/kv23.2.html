23.2 Of Bogus Arahants

Controverted Point: That infra-human beings, taking the shape of Arahants, follow sexual desires.

Theravādin: PTS cs 23.2.1Would you also say that such beings, resembling Arahants, commit any or all such crimes as are stated above (Kv 23.1)? You deny; but why limit them to one only of those crimes?
