• Points of Controversy

21.3 Of Certain Fetters

Controverted Point: That Arahantship is won without a certain “Fetter”-quantity being cast off.

Theravādin: PTS cs 21.3.1Do you imply that Arahantship is won without the extirpation of theory of soul, or doubt, or contagion of mere rule and ritual, or lust, or hate, or dullness, or indiscretion? You deny that you do, but your proposition cannot then be maintained.

PTS cs 21.3.2Or do you imply that the Arahant is prone to lust, hate, dullness, conceit, pride, despair, corruption? Is not the opposite true of him? How then can you say there are certain Fetters he has not cast off?

Mahāsaṅghika: PTS cs 21.3.3If I am wrong, tell me: does an Arahant know with the complete purview of a Buddha? You agree he does not. Hence I am right.
