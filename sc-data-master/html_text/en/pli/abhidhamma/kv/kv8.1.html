• Points of Controversy

8.1 Of Divers Destinies

Controverted Point: That there are six spheres of destiny.

Theravādin: PTS cs 8.1.1Did not the Exalted One name five destinies—purgatory, the animal kingdom, the Peta-realm, mankind, the devas? PTS cs 8.1.2 And did not the Kālakañjaka Asuras, who resembled the Petas in ugly or frightful shape, sex-life, diet, and length of life, intermarry with them? PTS cs 8.1.3 And did not Vepacitti's troop, who in the same respects resembled the devas, intermarry with devas? PTS cs 8.1.4 And had not Vepacitti's troop been formerly devas?

Andhaka, Uttarāpathaka: PTS cs 8.1.5But since there is an Asura-group, it is surely right to speak of it as a possible destiny?
