13.10 Of Desire for Ideas and the Cause of Ill

Controverted Point: That the natural desire for objects of mind is not the Cause of Ill.

Theravādin: PTS cs 13.10.1What reason have you for dissociating this form of craving from the other five? If you admit that a craving for objects of sight, sound, and so on, is immoral, you must admit as much concerning the co-ordination of these as ideas (percepts or images).

PTS cs 13.10.2–5Continue to imitate the preceding argument, Kv 13.9.
