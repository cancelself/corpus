Nuns’ Expiation (Pācittiya) 50

Bi-Pc.50 … at Sāvatthī in the Jeta Grove in Anāthapiṇḍika’s monastery. Now at that PTS vp Pali 4.306 time the group of six nuns taught worldly knowledge. People … spread it about … Bi-Pc.44.1. Instead of learn read teach. … “… this rule of training:

“Whatever nun should teach worldly knowledge, there is an offence of expiation.”

Bi-Pc.50.2.1 Whatever means: … nun is to be understood in this case.

Worldly knowledge means: …

Should teach means: if she teaches by line … Bi-Pc.44.2. Instead of learns read teaches. … if she is the first wrong-doer.
